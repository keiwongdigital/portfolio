rollCase
You may not be collecting business cards now like in years past? Right? rollCase apps gives you easiest way to digitize your business cards. The personal card case are always with you, any time you'll need it. whether it's from a face-to-face meeting or social event.
Client In-house
Scope iOS APP
Role UI Design / UX task

Objective

Nowadays, the people maintenance business cards inefficient, outdated and not eco-friendly. Receiving a physical business card is it can be easy to lose, The truth is, no one has the time to organize contacts and manually save business card details to their mobile. Also, we can not make a business card instantly.

Goals

Aim to design a business card app that puts the needs of users where they can intuitively find it. Helping you spend less time on manual entry and maintenance and more time connecting with your customers. The app platform makes designing a card simple, convenient, and more eco-friendly to help reducing paper waste.

Role

As a project of UX/UI Design, I was responsible for bringing rollCase to life from concept to high-fidelity prototypes, using research, ideation, design, testing, and interaction.

Design Process

Define and Ideate

At the beginning of the project, I started quick researches to better understand the goal & user needs. We have interviewed 3 peoples who use physcial card and card app on a daily bases. Each with their pain point and the way of organize.

Here are the common issues faced:
→ Easy to lose
→ lack of time
→UI ugly visual

To better understand not only our end-to-end users, I researched other cards system their service structure, target users, and app/web product. Through these researches, we are clarified market position and things that make up the core of the product.

After rounds of brainstorming and design iterations with my team member! We keep the core features as simple and intuitive user interface to smoothly finish their tasks that will appeal to the main target group.

We map out solution are:

  • Convert paper cards to digial version avoid everything in manually
  • Provide convenient way to exchange and organize business cards
  • Feel like a real cardcase and clear visual representation
  • Share digital business card with anyone, even if they do not have the app

User flow

After understanding what problems we are needed, I built a flow diagram that will help us make sure we have a solution for each of them. Here are the basic primciple of using the app to arhieve the user's goal.

Design

01_Wireframe

At this point, I started with wireframes of the detail page, so we could understand what each screen contains and how to move from one to the other. I tested the wireframes with 3 interviewees to understand which they preferred in terms of search and discovery, extracting information, and visual appeal. This guided how I designed the rest of the experience.

02_Style

We decided three keywords for rollcase visual style: Trendy, lively, and Clearly. Based on that, we finalized the color strategy, font choice and created the style guide. The annotated high-fidelity prototypes were created as the final deliverable.
