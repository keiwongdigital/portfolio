2nd Burger House Branding
The 2nd burger joint is a hand-crafted burger house joint serving during London's night time hours and is extremely well suited to the nightlife, where the product is not the deciding factor, but rather a brand that connects with the customer and with the small community culture is most important for the success of 2nd.
Deliverable Brand Identity
Role Brand Logo, Visual Assets and Social Media Campaigns

Design Context

The Story

The shop owner who is South Africans Tribe people live in UK, started as a hamburger restaurant at Arsenal Riverside after moving to the London. His own hand-crafted patties with south africa tribe spices marinate methods. The client needed a design brand identity to convey the pleasurable experience between traditional rustic tasty and community to costumers.

Brand Value

chatShop Owner said :

""Actually, burger sauces and spices are key, which are made by my country's cooking method. hmmm, I wanna say open at Night hour only..... Having say that, compared with other competing burger houses, this may be our most difference."

Ideating the Brand Personality

Determine values and message align the brand story

Logomark

The South Africa Tribe Mask is one of the most important elements. The fluidity of the line movements served as a great inspiration when designing the brand's logo. So, The main idea of the logo was mixing tribe mask and burger outline that creates casual and fluid hand-sketching outline, that evokes the brand's natural, organic and care-free ethos. Building a personality within the brand logo while still for community people interested in consuming the burger in a South Africans traditional spices.

Logotype

The brand name "2nd" comes from "Starting a new day at night. I wanted to embody this initial concept through the night." The letters of "2nd" are interconnected and fused into a symbol that is clearly readable and contrasting at night. All of these elements embody the brand message.

Typography and Color Palette

The two type font alternations add a care-free and reliable feel to the brand. Likewise, color palette that supports the overall project mood. I wishes to convey "Time" as a direct reference to the initial concept: timeless black-and-white colour combination portrays it’s presented night and day transitions, and night blue as sub-colors. The kraft texture which are applied to mix various visual basic elements.
