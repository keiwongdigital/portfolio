Goals

Reimagining a themed-bookstore app for Jin-Yong's novels lovers would like to give a more user-centric approach, Offer user-relevant features that bring delight fulfilling their hobby in reading. The second goal of the project was building a product responsive to the needs of prospective users.

There are 3 main aspects i could focus on:

  • Consistency - Establish a Themed-Bookstore to aligned with prospective users ambition
  • Usability - Propose a more engaging experience so that users are comfortable accessing novel.
  • Functionality - Offers a comfortably reading experience with its extensive features.

Role

This was an individual project. Though I am not in the publishing business, I did however like the idea of re-designing Jin-Yong novels e-reader. I take full of the roles involved in designing a product UX and UI.
