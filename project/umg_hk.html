Defining the Why?

Problem

Nowdays, A music audience's first impression of brand is based almost exclusively on digital platform. Currently, A Universal Music Group website leaves a lot to be desired, Meanwhile, over 68% bounce rate indicative of poor user experience throughout the entire website. If audience don’t understand the website purpose and what value it brings, they will leave. Why itʼs so important to improve their online presence to be more competitive in the evolving market.

Solution

The main goal was reconstruct content strategy the ways in which adding incentive for users to explore and quality of content it produces, within the website in order to increase engagement, on-site time and consider future scalability base on affiliate program. In the Meanwhile, Re-evaluate online image and experience to align with the brand consistently.

Research
