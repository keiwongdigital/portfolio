Overview

Redesigned the music platform with the aim of improving its digital presence in music brand and increasing music audience engagement and trust to be more competitive in terms of online experience.

Our partnership model : Partnering with UMG Hong Kong on an affiliate program to driven more CD sales. Here are the framework of the role :

  • Affiliate Creator - Universal Music Group who provide product and content
  • Affiliate Marketer - We establish platform that through an affiliate tracking links of CD album of primary product will direct consumers to UMG e-store (Ushop.com) to purchasing.

Design Context

The Problem Statement

Universal Music Group's website leaves a lot to be desired from the digital portal. The most obvious issue is the high 68% bounce rate, which indicates a poor user experience across the site and affects traffic on the CD store platform.

Nowdays, A music audience's first impression of brand is based almost exclusively on digital platform. If audience don’t understand the website purpose and what value it brings, they will leave.

Solution

The goal was reconstruct content architecture the ways in which adding incentive for users to explore and quality of content it produces, within the website in order to increase engagement, on-site time and consider scalability and attractability base on affiliate program.

As a primary design goal, reframe the structure of the content to make it easier to navigate and keep the audience around for longer.

Beside, Non-responsive design as main factor causes of high bounce rates. Able to across all device viewports was important which is a fast ways to reduce bounce rate and boost up website traffic also.

Crafting the Design System

Design Element & Visual Language

One of the design goals was to more closely align the brand. I outlined 2 keywords: "Modernity" and "Legibility" are design principal of the website.

I determined the new visual direction and mood boards where are pins of style, typography, and color schemes that aligned with my vision for UMG guideline.

Utilized dark theme and Noto sans font family is in-depth read for infomative content and highly contrast. An expressive, I also created a set of vector text element and CD mockup, where intention of letting text and images flesh out the design and recognizable easily as main CD product.

Plus, I created the UI Kit that worked between teamate and developers handoff. Having organized library of components has increased teamate work efficiency and accuracy.

Iterative Design and Hierarchy

With the rise in the complexity of projects, and the how to better interpret that for the design interface. I know carry out a brand refresh at the same time as a website redesign. The 2nd design it was greater emphasis on feel, music audiences connection, content and visual balance will be beneficial to user journey.

Throught few round revisions of technical issue and A/B testing with the developer for website structure and image load whether a content presented will be favorable to UMG brand. The client team was also invited to participate in the testing to find out which one performs better. Finally we had our final design.
