Conversion-focused on Landing

Landing influence users perception of a UMG branding at first sight. Having two design goal was clear on the landing page: 1. Engage social media followers or subscribers, 2. Drive more sales to Ushop.com. In order to capture audience attention faster when landed on. The final version was devised three sections only that immediately connect the message with the visuals as well as ensure easy discoverability.

Here, I designed a card-centric components as seem like "大字報" that clearly showcasing thier content, where consist of foucus published CD album, Artist's social feed and upcoming event. There are different styles with recognize tag of various content increasing content hierarchy and makes it easy to proceed to the next step of the users journey which will lead to a reduce bouncing rate also.

Having a full screen menu also helps in this aspect as it provides a clear overview of the website, providing easy access to the rest of the content.

Affiliate program was second goal of the project. As you see, once the user clicks on CTA button in Album Detail page. The affiliate tracking link puts a ID parameter redirect to Ushop.com (official e-shop) with unique ID number is used to track the sale back to website, when user purchase the CD Album.

In terms of searching that not being redirected to a new page where results are loaded. The results will be suitable options is displayed below which leaves the chance for users to change any of their search criteria without leaving the page. At that point, providing visual feedback and autocomplete under the search bar that has suggestions for queries based on whatever the user has typed.
