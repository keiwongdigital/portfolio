Identifiable designs was needed especially music brand importantly. Establish artist social media feed wall and upcoming event instead of overwhelme artist's news in the website. The soical feed can provides variety content in real-time, keep the website fresh via a steady stream of social content thereby giving a boost to engagement.

Re-ideating of Information Architecture

In order to create more organized content, I reorganized upon original the content architecture, modifyed the similar and overload infomtaion to streamline website navigation. Here, I sketched out the simple wireflow which helped us quickly get a rough concepts.

There are now five section in the revised which are HOME, UMG PRESS, GENRE, MEDIA and ABOUT. I kept it quite simple because avoid user journeys involve drilling down through several level. I created new ‘UMG PRESS’ page that contain 3 sub-section are :

  • 焦點新聞 which is combined local and international news
  • 藝人社交 where are embeded social wall feature that can find all the latest UMG artist feed
  • 注目動態 where are artist upcoming event on social media, that contains facebook live, concert info and ablum release activity

Due to time constraints of project, Clients want to see how overall design will look on a finished website as possible. However, I jumping straight into designing prototyping, speed it up project processing to deliver the promised quality.
