Universal Music Group (Hong Kong) Revamp
Universal Music Group. is the world class in music-based entertainment, with a broad array of businesses engaged in recorded music, music publishing and audiovisual content in more than 60 countries.
Deliverable Website
Role UX - Ideation, IA, Content Strategy
UI - Design System, Visual Design, Prototypes, Front-end Coding

Design Context

Overview

The original website lacked relevance and failed to effectively communicate the value to music audiences, hindering its competitive advantage.

Redesigned the music platform to rebuild trust with music audiences and enhance its online presence. Increased the sales potential of the CD e-shop through an affiliate programme to make it more competitive among Hong Kong music labels.

Here is the framework for the Affiliate Programme Partnership :

  • Affiliate Creator - Universal Music Group who providing product and content
  • Affiliate Marketer - We establish platform that through an affiliate tracking links of CD album of primary product will direct consumers to UMG e-store (Ushop.com) to purchase.

Unfortunately, there is no UX role to conduct in-depth research and measure business impact to prove the value of affiliate marketing programs.

The Problem

Universal Music Group's website failed to communicate its value to the music audiences. The most obvious problem is the high bounce rate of 68%, which indicates a poor experience across the site and affects access traffic to the CD eShop platform.

Nowdays, A music audience's first impression of brand is based almost exclusively on online content. If audience don't understand the website purpose and what value it brings, they will leave.

What's Design Challenge

"How might we re-design the website experience to reduce the bounce rate problem, as well as to retain users and drive more traffic to the official CD eShop?"

The Solution

We re-architect the content architecture with targeted placement of content to increase the music audience's motivation to explore and stay on the site longer. Meanwhile, By reinforce the online presence to express with the brand values to increase the music audience's intent to sell CDs in the affiliate programme.

Discovery and Research

Inital Finding

After the kick-off meeting with the client to find out the most crucial issue and expectation. Through Google Analytics research where are realised the bouncing rate of current website is too high OVER 68%, thus, which leading to a significant drop in consumers who have moved from official website to thier CD e-Shop (Ushop.com). The major problem was clearly.

After few round meetings with stakeholders, We conducted an online survey with UMG marketing team involving 43 people to learn more current website what happened.

Problem Analysis

Here, I brainstormed with teamate gathered survey responses and analysed the current website. We then evaluated the results into 3 overarching issue to helping us to narrow down the scope of the project, and learn how each of them impact:

Overall experience was negative

55%

Respondents left in a short time when they landed without clicking anywhere else on the site, and they also expressed an outdated design.

tips_and_updatesTakeaway : 
The truth is, whole website was non responsive design and discovered about 45% of traffic comes from mobile, in which are factor they leave that causes of a high bounce rate, and hurts Ushop.com traffic as well. Beside, current website was not align with brand consistency and personality that hurts brand image and trust.

Poorly structured and loose website

42%

Respondents mentioned that they felt the site's clutter affected their browsing experience, and also mentioned that they got the wrong results when searching for CD.

tips_and_updatesTakeaway : 
Unclear content hierarchy is currently a major problem. Content too dense and busy. Artist news and CD album promo banners are flooding the site, replicated everywhere where it doesn't immediately look like a proper music site tone. They only care if they can find the content they want. In addition, CD purchase intent was reduced due to the poor search function.

Lack of social media presence

40%

Respondents expressed frustration at missing artists' live performances simply because they can't find the information. They said they come here to look for artists' Facebook Live or activity information.

tips_and_updatesTakeaway : 
Nothing connections and features about social media. Existing website does not match up with what music audience was expecting and connecting: Havent social media content that they interact with it, which would just turn off the users interest or keep browsing.

Market Research

I did an analysis of competitors on similar music brand, the differences between various of the content and product music audience bases, as well as ways that our design could stand out from the competition in the marketplace.

Ideating the Design Framework

Identify the Design Goals

We identified the problems we were currently facing and prioritised them according to our findings. Here are points of view to make design decisions after teammates brainstorm.

As a Music Audience :

- To see a well structured website that reflects music label professionalism and trustworthy

- To view artist content that is interesting and useful

As a Brand Owner :

- To be maintaining industry status as one of Hong Kong's influential music labels

- Artists are crucial assets for a music label's to increase CD purchase intent

tips_and_updatesINSIGHTS :

Apart from redesigning websites, communicating values and beliefs clearly and intuitively is critical for a major brand, and equally important for maintaining a long-term vision.

Therefore, this makes core value and content delivery a primary design objective.

The Solution

Re-construct the website and content architecture

As a primary design goal, reframe the website structure of the content to make it easier to navigate and keep the audience around for longer. Beside, non-responsive design as the main factor causing high bounce rates. Being able to work across all device viewports was important, which is a quick way to reduce bounce rates.

browser_updated

Focusing landing experience to leads conversion

Make it as clear as possible at first glance what the targeted content is. Evoke action and emotion quickly, thereby reduce drop off when landing on the site and boost up conversions opportunities.

network_check

Consistent with the tone of the music brand

Provide a more recognizable design that that aims to encourage users interacts with. With a strong visual appearance was particularly important for the music brand.

shape_line

Reduce gap between brand and target audiences

Create an artist's social feed wall feature to maintain social engagement and keep the site fresh with a steady stream of social content.

Create an artist's events page to keep track of upcoming concerts or live events, and also interaged with the social calendar feature to keep track of events they're interested in.

Re-framing Content Structure

I sketched out the draft which helped us to figure out the new structure what needed to be deleted, merged or added. We identifed topics and keywords of interest to them with Google Analytics Insights what needed to be tweaked in the landing page and client consideration also.

Unlike the old site flow, the top levels of menu items have been streamlined from seven to five by content review, which are: HOME, UMG PRESS, GENRE, MEDIA and ABOUT.

A new release centre called "UMG PRESS" at the first level of the website structure, which combines all releases, both official news and UMG artist content.

  • NEWS -  Combined artist's news content of local and international
  • SOCIAL FEED -  Shows artist's social media post
  • EVENT -  Shows artist's upcoming event information

Establishing the Design Language

Visual Communication

One of the design goals was to more closely align the brand. I outlined 2 keywords: "Modernity" and "Legibility" are design principal of the website.

I determined the new visual direction and mood boards where are pins of style, typography, and color schemes that aligned with my vision for UMG guideline.

Utilized dark theme and Noto sans font family is in-depth read for infomative content and highly contrast. An expressive, I also created a set of CD type visual element and CD mockup, where intention of letting text and images flesh out the design and recognizable easily as main CD product.

Plus, I created the UI Kit that worked between teamate and developers handoff. Having organized library of components has increased teamate work efficiency and accuracy.

Iterate the Design

With the rise in the complexity of projects, and the how to better interpret that for the design interface. I know carry out a brand refresh at the same time as a website redesign. The 2nd design it was greater emphasis on feel, music audiences connection, content hierarchy and visual balance will be beneficial to user journey.

Throught few round revisions of technical issue and A/B testing with the developer for website structure and image load whether a content presented will be favourable to UMG brand. The client team was also invited to participate in the testing to find out which one performs better. Finally we had our final design.
