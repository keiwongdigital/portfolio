Project Goals

Develop comprehensive music platform that helps UMG Hong Kong achieve digital goals:

  • Redefine the ways in which content can be discovered and consumed within the website in order to increase engagement and dwell time, reduce its bounce rate
  • Elevate the overall experience to align with the brand and the quality of content it produces.

The second goal of the project: A partnership of affiliate program with UMG Hong Kong to driven more sales, Here are the framework of the role:

  • Affiliate Creator - UMG who provide product and content
  • Affiliate Marketer - Establishing platform that through an affiliate tracking links in album detail page will direct consumers to UMG album e-store (Ushop.com) to checkout.

DISCOVERY

Problem

First, We formed up teams with the client to identify the most crucial issues, and which content needed to be improved. Meanwhile, We conducted with 8 people online interviews rapidly to evaluate the current website what the problems their are facing. The questions provided a framework for our research, and user needs. Here are three key finding after evaluate:

Unstructured content reduce the discoverability

Poor content design and structure with not relevant to users needs, We discoved overload of similar information and lengthy content scrolling in the home page. interviewees mentioned that usually got incomplete result of the album when search they wanted, which affect users when they are shopping online intent.

Negative experience increase bounce rate

Whole website was outdated, non mobile-friendly, inconsistency of branding and hence they left, which resulting in frustrated users, hurts SEO ranking of the brand as well.

Lack of social media presence

Base on result, Some people in interviews expressed frustration about missing artist's live simply because they didn’t remember to seek out this info. They said coming here to look for artist's facebook live or activities. They would like to track events. However, the current displaying artist news and album only. There are impact of interacting with target audience.

Competitive Analysis

During the discovery phase, I did an analysis of direct and indirect competitors, the differences between various of the content and product music audience bases, I wanted to understand why certain audience chose their platforms, and what kinds of features and content should offer, and why they left.

Final Thoughts

The project was very big and fun for me. As a designer, it's not just screen design. I learnt the role visual design plays in reflecting the mission and values of a music production brand. It’s extremely important to strike a balance between brand image and actual visual design.

The clients have a vision. But they don't always have the UX knowledge to understand users. Sometimes their requirements conflict with users needs. It's our designers' roles to help them empathize with users and bridge their vision with users' needs, and get on-board with a holistic design solution.
