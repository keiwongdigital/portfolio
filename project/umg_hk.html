Project Goals

Develop comprehensive music platform that helps UMG Hong Kong achieve digital goals :

  • Redefine the ways in which content by highlighting essential content, adding incentive for users to explore, within the website in order to increase engagement and dwell time, reduce its bounce rate.
  • Elevate the overall experience to align with the online brand images and the quality of content it produces.

  • Affiliate Creator - UMG who provide product and content
  • Affiliate Marketer - Establishing platform that through an affiliate tracking links in album detail page will direct consumers to UMG album e-store (Ushop.com) to checkout.

Problem Statement

"How might we re-design the UMG digital online experience, reduce its bounce rate, as well as increasing retention of existing users?"

DISCOVERY

Problem

First, We formed up teams with the UMG marketing team to identify the most crucial issues, and which content needed to be improved. Meanwhile, We conducted with 10 people online interviews to evaluate the current website by survey. The questions provided a framework what the problems their are facing. Here are three key finding after evaluate:

  • Unstructured content reduce the discoverability
    It is clear; Poor content design and structure with not relevant to users needs, We discoved overload of similar information and lengthy content scrolling in the home page. interviewees mentioned that usually got incomplete result of the album when search they wanted, which affect users when they are shopping online intent.
  • Negative experience increase bounce rate
    The truth hurts is whole website was outdated, non mobile-friendly, inconsistency of branding and hence they left, which resulting in frustrated users, hurts SEO ranking of the brand as well.
  • Lack of social media presence
    Base on result, Some people in interviews expressed frustration about missing artist's live simply because they didn’t remember to seek out this info. They said coming here to look for artist's facebook live or activities. They would like to track events. However, the current displaying artist news and album only. There are impact of interacting with target audience.

Competitive Analysis

During the discovery phase, I did an analysis of direct and indirect competitors, the differences between various of the content and product music audience bases, I wanted to understand why certain audience chose their platforms, and what kinds of features and content should offer, and why they left.

DESIGN

Information Architecture

In order to create more organized content, We are reorganized upon original the content architecture, combined the duplicated section. And give a new name to the old section. I sketched out the simple wireflow which helped us quickly get an idea and give a rough concepts. I tried to avoid the unapplicable design based on front-end developing skill.

There are now five section in the revised which are ‘HOME’ ‘UMG PRESS’ ‘GENRE’ ‘MEDIA’ and ‘ABOUT’.

Site Map

Avoid deep navigation, I created new ‘UMG Press’ section contain as sub tag: ‘焦點新聞’ is which is combined local and international news. ‘藝人社交’ where are embeded social wall feature that can find all the latest update on artist SNS. ‘注目動態’ where are artist upcoming event on social media, such as facebook live, concert content and ablum release activity.

Due to time constraints, I skipp wireframing phase, jumping straight into visual stage and high-fidelity prototyping. This will also spend much time for the visual stage.

Final Thoughts

The project was very big and fun for me. As a designer, it's not just screen design. I learnt the role visual design plays in reflecting the mission and values of a music production brand. It’s extremely important to strike a balance between brand image and actual visual design.

The clients have a vision. But they don't always have the UX knowledge to understand users. Sometimes their requirements conflict with users needs. It's our designers' roles to help them empathize with users and bridge their vision with users' needs, and get on-board with a holistic design solution.
