Role

Over the project of 3 months, I was part of the core team, where i focused on UI visual design and part of front-end coding as well producing all major deliverables. As the only designer in this project collaborating closely with project manager, development teammate and UMG marketing team to develop the website ensure the best outcome.

Challenges

First of all, Identify their current website problem and UMG most concerned how to reach thier business goals through affiliate platforms. Secondly, research other music brand would set them apart from their competitors brand image and convey their business message. So i have defined main task are:

  • Keep it simple which help design feel structured. Make it easy to explore and better experiences
  • Visually appealing website to keep visitors as long as possible
  • Increase audience engagement and purchasing intent
  • Responsive design across all device was needed

Design Process

While designing whole website, I took care to create a style that was generally simple, that would emphasize the album cover especially. It has to look visually appealing while maintaining a seamless and intuitive user experience. The landing page would say it all and as user scroll down, they would exactly know what this whole website is about. Most importantly, An engaged online audience is more likely to make purchasing decisions. In addition, embeded social feed wall feature can website no need publish new content also keep it fresh increase the user engage.

01_Ideation

First, I sketching some user journey and content architecture which helped us quickly get an idea and give a rough concepts. We realized some of the ideas and layouts that we were thinking of just wouldn't work.

02_Wireframeing

Once we collected core content, I translated into low-fi wireframes from inital sketch to build the quick look, key features and user flow to gather initial feedback from the team. Out of that, to make sure every flow was complete and the tasks order were logical which could more discussions, intial test, iterate on the outcome and annotated them.

03_Design Guide

Determined the flow and team feedback. Then, I created mood boards on Photoshop where algin pins of style, typography, and color schemes that aligned with my vision for clients. I approached the whole visual concept was clean, unique and attractive.

Grid

While designing, used 1600px container could effective for album cover that it involves how to use much album cover in one screen efficiently and make it adaptive as well

Color

Dark themed which can express a wider range of color and depth. Also enhance album cover contrast which important for the music site. Used high gradient contrast for CTA button stand out from the background

Customize Visual

As for the icon set, I have created that minimal and simple, that the user can easily understand. In addition, create a set of calligraphy visual to whole website are important to make the users anchored. These visual can be used in several places such as section title, album detail, bio making the characteristic of the website more coherent

04_Visual Language

From this phase, I moved onto high-fidelity prototyping, Need to think and reassemble the contents to keep things consistent across all the elements in the new design.


Then we determined a new visual direction after a round of usability testing. I dedicated time and effort in designing each UI modular which its associated user interaction to ensure easy usability. In addtion, creating a component library which flexible for reusable as the website grows and also provide a highly efficient way to build the site.

Takeaway

The project was very big and fun for me. As a designer, it's not just screen design. I learnt the role visual design plays in reflecting the mission and values of a music production brand. It’s extremely important to strike a balance between brand image and actual visual design. Both are equally important when you’re designing for a user.

Also, I realizing how to best prepare the assets, design elements and system so that the teammate could understand easily, reducing the amount of time for back-and-forth communication and adjustment.

The whole experiencing opened my eyes to the difficulties and joys of creating digital products.
