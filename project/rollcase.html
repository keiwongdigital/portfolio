roll:Case
Digital business card platform designed to organise business cards more efficiently in a stress-free, user-centric way to go digital and reduce the environmental impact of paper use.
Deliverable APP
Role UX - Research, Ideation, Wireframe, Usability Testing
UI - Design System, Visual Design, Interaction Design, Prototypes

Design Context

Overview

Due to COVID-19, there has been a shift towards everything digital and sustainability. However, this is a solo project that I takes full UX/UI, and I was inspired by an environmental news story. The aim is to eliminate people's dependence on paper business cards and a huge drain on the earth's resources which may be unnecessary.

The aim was adapt to user behaviour and reduce the time spent on app by optimising the overall digital experience, whether it's a paper or the current business cards user. The high vision was "PAPERLESS" to help people be more eco-friendly; "Save Our Home".

Did you know ??

"Each year around 100 Billion business cards are produced worldwide, Has over 7 million trees are cut down to print paper business cards. 58% end up in the bin, and going to landfill."

The Problem

Business cards are still a business courtesy in our society, but the fact that they are not environmentally friendly. It is unlikely that business cards will be phased out any time soon.

Obviously, many people still use paper business cards. Whenever business cards are sorted, many of them end up in the trash which causes pollution.

On the other hand, the current app experience steps are vague and difficult which creating barriers for users. This means that users reuse paper business cards.

Design Challenge

"How might we reduce barriers for paper card users going digital in order to achieve environmental goals?"

"How might we design a stress-free task flow to well-organize business cards for current user more efficiency?"

The Solution

Establishing easy-to-digest task flows that guide users to take action more easily will reduce the cognitive load and stress on both types of users. This is critical to increasing their motivation to card digitize.

Knowing the Business card users facing

The first things was to understand the users through data. To better understand the target audience and scope, I started with research. I interviewed 10 participants in Facebook and Whatsapp chat groups by survey about how they manage and collect business cards on a daily basis.

The following questions to learn about their current user facing :

  • Are you a paper business card user?
  • What do you do when you receive a client's business card?
  • How often do you take the time to organise your clients' business cards? How much time do you spend?
  • If you have a those app experience, How much time to import? How helpful were those apps to you?
  • What do you like or dislike about those App / Paper business card? Tell me about past experiences.

Ideating the Design Strategy

Proposed Solution

Streamline step-oriented make tasks run smoothly

In my proposal, I honed on streamline the task flow so that users won't have to face any additional steps such as manual import and card appearance design which were previously seen as barriers and stuck.

Beside, enhance step flow visually to guide that helps reduce users cognitive load and frustrations. It also helps to heighten the motivation of both user types. I suggested these 3 actionable solutions :

developer_mode

Simpify creation for digital card

Create flow intergated OCR feature that able to capture paper card data by camera shot its as a digital card appearance also. That able to skip manually input and design card step- ideal for those who are paper card users, and who are unaware of design.

phonelink_ring

Improve usability through NFC exchange processes

Creating an automated process for NFC data exchange makes it easier for users to organise their cards in one place after an exchange. Avoid manual updates with an update request when other side change their card information.

Creating a schedule and reminder feature keeps things in the future, so there is no need to use other apps to make a schedule. This way, encourages them to use the app more often as a habit.

I placing the persona "Daniel" in an scenario helps identify the functions. This purpose of considering NFC exchange to happen, and how interact in thier busy routine in this storyboard.

contacts

Digital cardcase approach for easier interaction

A home screen similar to a cardcase gives them immersion rather than just a contact list. Make the interactions the same as the paper cardcase to ensures real-life usage and behaviour.

User Flow

The previous user flow had quite a few fuzzy and stuck steps to get through. Such as after create could contact person info, they had to design card visual part. This flow wasn't really helpful for people in fragmented time who needed complete quickly there's room to simplify the process.

Digitalize Paper Card

The newly proposed card digitalize process is all about avoid importing information to speed up their work flow or where users get stuck, so that the app can be quickly operated by simply moving a finger on one hand.

Create Own Card

In the new flow of creating my own cards, I did away with all the vague parts that didn't directly contribute to the task flow objective as much as possible. Such as an unnecessary description that is replaced with an icon.
