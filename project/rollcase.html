roll:Case
You may not be collecting business cards now like in years past? Right? roll:Case apps gives you easiest way to digitize your business cards. The digital card case are always with you, any time you'll need it. Whether it's from a face-to-face meeting or social event.
Deliverables iOS APP
Scope UX - Strategy, Wireframing, User Testing / UI - Design, Prototyping

Project Goals

Develop a digitize card platform aims to implement features and interactions to create a fresh and smoothly experience for business users. Key task abilities all in one spot. Total Visibility into business card and contact effortlessly, grow professional. Also, We believe it could reduce the entry barrier to physical card user and, in that way, increase our potential market.

The highly project vision was paperless, "Save a tree", "Save Mother Earth". Did you know? 8 billion of business cards we print each year end up in the bin within a week.

DISCOVERY

Research

The first thing to do is to get to know the users through data. We started researches to better understand the goal and user needs. We have interviewed 13 peoples from facebook who use physcial card and card app on a daily bases. Each with their frustration and the way of use. Here are the common issues faced:

Physical card users :

  • Lack of time to organize
    -- "I always keep cards in my suits pocket after meeting" --
    -- "Lose? Misplace? End up wasting time to find card on my card case" --
    -- "Sometimes Im not sure who give it to me" --
  • Environmental Pollution / Cost increase
    -- "If i just moved office or changed something? re-print card?" --

11/13 of interviewees said still use phyiscal card. They have been in business a long time already, and store sizable stash of business cards that need to get organized. They feel impatient and extra work.

Digital card app users :

  • User-unfriendly
    -- "I use app recently but I dislike complex step when i import the card" --
    -- "Not enough hints how to done it, It was hard to operate" --
  • Not offer much functionality
    -- "Paper card limited in size, sometimes i use Google Keep or paper drop notes." --
    -- “I want to get notified when a meeting before. Reminder is important for me, so I can start the preparing quickly." --
    -- "I wanna to place the contact info position into left corner, No option how to adjust" --
    -- "Can't share it to my workmate after template created" --

5/13 of interviewees used current app in the market but they have a bad experience. In term of usability, Feel frustrated on complex step and without guide in action performing their tasks. Also, they mentioned extra app usage for thier task.

Key Insight

#1 How they currently business card management

Organizing the business cards after meeting is major pain point of the both user digital and physical card, but always have been hassle work, The truth is, no one has the spend time to organize contacts and manually save business card details to their mobile or put into cardcase. In terms of the useability, They complained that the process feels complex and long. Additionally, in-app features not fufill users request currently during the real-life needs also.

#2 Their dont worries impact of card lossing

We discovered a huge user still use the physical card today are most importantly. People can not make a business card instantly if someone’s information changed they had to order an entirely new stack of business cards. There's additional environmental waste when the people lose the card and production of the card also.

Competitive Analysis

We began by doing some research to see what’s out there currently. I noted down the popular platforms, identified the key features I ideated rollcase to have, and reassessed the existing platforms based on those features.

The majority of the features between competitiors were very similar, however the main differences that we noticed were:

  • Easily accessible -vs- Hardly accessible

DEFINE

Challenge

How might we design a trusting, stress-free digitalize business cards app for busy-man and engage physical card user going digital as well?

Throughout a few brainstorming with teamate, created an affinity diagram to identify the project scope and evaluated of the result. We are ideating two main issue of the solution:

#1

Re-ideating task flow enhances experiences

High task completion rate by keeping main interactions at a minimum. Focuses on visualising operation that able to lessening users mental workload and cognitive load. Through step-based solution and an amiable approach to the emotions of users. Interactions and visual design are simple, stripping down the complex steps section is key.

  • Focuses on visualising operation without any design skill
    Streamline the create own cards flow that able to lead them for further action decsion easily. Meanwhile, provide build-in template that speed up the process to lessening users mental workload.
  • Shifting manage card habits after meeting
    Exchange business card process are presented in a digestible way that resembles real life. intuitive motion within 2 step - Open it and share it. Once a card exchanged, allow user keep track of schedules, or set reminders for the future things, avoid extra app usage.
  • Eliminate manually work from physical card to digitalize
    By in-App scanner feature auto-fill text field and editable format after extract data. Reduce user frustration of manage card, shifting physical card user thinking also.

#2

Effective Dashboard increases convenience

Base on the result, A user would be expect the same logic as the organize thier business card they previously used. Establishing a "Dashboard" like as real-life cardcase where as card hub overview and all key task in one spot, to better displaying what it is meant to be showing: CARD! which improving its feature access and relevant thier onward journey easily than to describe it with pure of contact list.
