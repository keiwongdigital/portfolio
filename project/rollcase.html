roll:Case
You may not be collecting business cards now like in years past? Right? roll:Case gives you easiest way to digitize your business cards. The digital card case are always with you any time, Whether it's from a face-to-meeting or social event.
Deliverables iOS APP
Scope UX - Strategy, Research, Ideation / UI - Visual Design, Wireframe, Prototyping

Project Goals

Redesign a digitize card platform that aims to user-centric approach to create a seamlessly experience for business man. Reduce the entry barrier to physical card user through a streamlined process simultaneously, so that increasing the potential market. The highly vision of the project was Paperless, "Save a tree"; "Save Mother Earth". Did you know? Each year 100 billion business cards are produced worldwide, 58% end up in the bin, and going to landfill.

This app was my first project involed UX taskt I worked on, and new to UX field. I carried out an end-to-end process - from understanding the users real needs to implementing it.

Problem Statement

"How might we design a trusting, stress-free digitalize business cards app for business-man and engage physical card user going digital as well?"

DISCOVERY

Research

The first thing to do is to get to know the users through data. I started researches to better understand the goal. I conducted 13 interviewees which Facebook group vote poll, whatsApp group questionnaire who use physcial card and card app on a daily bases. Each with their frustration and the way of use. Here are the summarized issues thier faced:

Results

Physical Card Users :

  • Lack of time to organize

    • "I always keep cards in my suits pocket after meeting"

    "Lose? Misplace? End up wasting time to find card on my card case"

    "Sometimes Im not sure who give it to me"

  • Environmental Pollution / Cost increase

    • "If i just moved office or changed something? re-print card? How about the old one?"

11/13 of interviewees said who still using phyiscal card, but only 3 of those participants have used (or even heard) Digital card app. This opens plenty of rooms for development for Digital card app for potential users.

7/13 of interviewees who business-man a long time already, and store sizable stash of business cards that need to get organized. They feel impatient and extra work.

Digital Card App Users :

  • User-unfriendly interface

    • "I use app recently but I dislike complex step when i import the card"

    "Not enough hints how to done it, It was hard to operate"

  • Not offer much functionality

    • "Paper card limited in size, sometimes i use Google Keep or paper drop notes."

    “I want to get notified when a meeting before. Reminder is important for me, so I can start the preparing quickly."

    "I wanna to place the contact info position into left corner, No option how to adjust"

    "Can't share it to my workmate after template created"

5/13 of interviewees used current app in the market but they have a bad experience. In term of usability, Feel frustrated on complex step and without guide in action performing their tasks. Also, they mentioned extra app usage for thier task.

Key Insight

#1Point

How they currently business card management?

Organizing the business cards after meeting is major pain point of the both user digital and physical card, but always have been hassle work, The truth is, no one has the spend time to organize contacts and manually save business card details to their mobile or put into cardcase. In terms of the usability, They complained that the process feels complex and overwhelmed that decrease motivation and anticipation for users using the app. Additionally, in-app features not fufill users request currently during the real-life needs also.

#2Point

They dont worries influence of card lossing!

I discovered a huge user still use the physical card today are most importantly. People can not make a business card instantly if someone’s information changed they had to order an entirely new stack of business cards. There's additional environmental waste when the people lose the card and production of the card also.

Competitive Analysis

I began by doing some research to see what’s out there currently. I noted down the popular platforms, identified the key features I ideated rollcase to have, so that reassessed the existing platforms based on those features.

The major of the features between competitiors were very similar, however the main differences that i noticed were:

  • Hardly usability  -vs-  Smoothly access

SOLUTION

"What was the problem I were trying to solve? And how exactly does it apply?

Throughout a few round of ideating. Then, I created an affinity diagram to identify the project scope idea and evaluated of the result. Here are two major issue of the solution summarized result of previously phase:

#1

Re-ideating task flow enhances experiences

Through intuitive step-based solution that able to lessening users cognitive load. Stripping down the complex steps and approaching one-hand operations simultaneously, that ensure that fit well the real-life scenarios which reduce the entry barrier of physical card user to going digial, so that reduce paper card usage.

  • Focusing on step-by-step interaction
    Establish the more visually way of the digital card creation within three-step, that able to lead them for further action decsion easily. In order to avoid manually, that allow user importing from address book, mail and vCard format would be ensure fast and easy to apply. Meanwhile, provide ready-made template without any design skill that speed up the process, which almost fully automated actions.
  • Shifting organize card habits
    Exchange process are presented in a digestible way that resembles real life; 1. Open card you wanted share to; 2. Near by NFC-enabled or (same app) devices; which a fit well after meeting to motivate them to change how organize and manage of the card, reduce effort and time afterwards. Once a card exchanged, that allow user create the working schedules and reminders for the future things, make it following up easily avoid extra app usage.
  • Eliminate manually work from physical card to digitalize
    By in-App OCR scanner feature, that auto-fill text field and editable format after extract data. By doing this, to reduce user frustration of manual entry errors, Additionaly, reserve the images after taken card shot as a digital card appearance that able to skipped design card process if users unnecessary.

#2

Effective Digital Cardcase increases convenience

Base on the result, I intend to make same logic as the organize thier business card they previously used. Establishing a Home as a "Digital Cardcase" like as real-life where as card hub overview. All key task in one spot was accessible easily. This is great way to displaying what it is meant to be showing: CARD; which improving its feature access and relevant thier onward journey easily than to describe it with pure of contact list.

Create own card processing:

Scan card processing:

In terms of the scan card processing, Above is another example; testing showed users were unsure whether the correct part of the card shot was selected and edit after; Following its implementation, however, users were overlooking and missing the contact data beneath it. The Home as "Digital Cardcase" is the core screen of the app, where displaying the overview of current card status where were organizing and presenting "CARD" element easily. By default, lastest card is top of list. Tap on mid-bar quick filter panel allows a user to sort out contact list rapidly, where are sort type: recent contact, favorites, company, group etc. Tap on a card to show detail of the people. Users can swipe left phone number icon make a phone call directly. Additionally, user can add people on the contact list if they have a relationship. This features was inspired by drafts iterating phase. Let user follow up with thier personal network.

Designing interaction with vertical scrolling which way give users feel "Real" cardcase so that the same motion here. By placing the core feature button in an obvious location, which design frosted glass effect that incorporating card and dynamic visuals. The card are still blurred behind another when user scrolling, its more catchy.

As prototyping shown, A three step-based approach which is customizable and flexible to lead them to built up the digital card.

Once the user tap on a headshot they are taken to the "My card case" which is hub of own card. Within the create own card process where 3 mode to:

1. Start all over - Step-by-step creation
2. Corporate - Access from cloud storage after created
3. Scan card - Physical card by OCR scanner

Start all over

In step 1 - Contact Info where user can import from address book, mail and vCard format sync through thier phone, so that contact info is always up to date which is prevent manual data entry errors. also, user can adding head icon if needed.

In step 2 - Digital card visual creation is divided to two option:

- Customizable
Allow user customize the visual element with tools, cropping and color overlay etc

- Build-in template
Allow user to apply ready-made template, the system will be loaded with imported data that help to streamline the entire process

In step 3 - There's a card style setting that allow user adjust the card layout, text style and info display setting. Within as the user adjust their digital card, the outcome will be change in preview instanly. There are more flexible option for card detail setting for card what info displaying on.

Also, with enable "Set as corporate template", just one time created which cloud base storage, where share with workmate or teams. Allow user reuse card template content to standardize with thier brand guideline avoid wasting time duplicating efforts.

As mentioned previously, After users created first rollCase digital business card. It automated work almost completely meet users needs by NFC’s feature card exchanging. If the partner has rollCase app, even better.

As prototyping shown, swipe up center "Exchange" icon in the Home screen. Users can swiping digital card wanted exchange to if they are muiltipe card users. Once card exchanged, where you are when you share and receive contact information they can save it directly to their phone. In additional, share card via QR code (auto generated by card created) or vCard file format with the people even other person doesn’t the rollCase app.

Once exhanged, Home screen was showing new card. Then, by long tap the digital card where a manage action lets user edit, delete. It also comes with card share via email, WhatsApp, Facbook and Skype etc. Within the card detail page, where give user to add notes/schedule reminder with dates organizing activities if needed.

Tap on "Scan" button to launch in-app camera and take a shot of physical card, its a camera with OCR feature which can extract data form the card shot. Users just aim and tap to snap a photo, Then, the result thumbnails of your scanned cards on the top of the screen. With OCR processing which converts information such as names, titles, company name into a editable format, and if there is any detail that's incorrect, user can manually fix it by the thumbnails of the top.

Once info imported, "Keep the camera shot" option that reserved shot before as digital card, if the user not required visual of the card. From a user’s perspective it makes creating your business partner card an instanly and easily.

Onboarding animated illustrations that show the app’s core information. Let user know the app does and the features it includes and more guidance. They’ll want to know what the app can do for them in addition to how to do it and increases the attention and level of trust in it.
