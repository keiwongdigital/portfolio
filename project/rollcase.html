roll:Case
Even in digital era, and instant messaging very popular today, the exchange of business cards continues to be a business etiquette and social custom. As we’re unlikely to see business cards fade from the business landscape anytime soon, the roll:Case app gives users stress-free way to organize your business cards. Want to finally get those cards organized and well control?
Deliverables iOS APP
Scope UX - Strategy, Research, Ideation / UI - Visual Design, Wireframe, Prototyping

Project Goals

Establish a digitize card platform that aims to user-centric approach to create a more effinient experience for business-man. In the meanwhile, to reduce the entry barrier to physical card user through a streamlined process simultaneously so that increasing the potential market.

The highly vision was "PAPERLESS" to bring extra value on the project, "Save a tree"; "Save Mother Earth". Did you know? Each year 100 billion business cards are produced worldwide, 58% end up in the bin, and going to landfill.

DISCOVERY

Research

The first thing to do is to get to know the users through data. I started researches to better understand the goal. I conducted 15 interviewees which facebook and whatsapp group quick survey questions who was manage, gather business card with a client on a daily bases.

The following questions to learn about their current user facing:

  • How do you handle it when your get the business card from the client?
  • How do you currently organize your client business card? How much time spend on?
  • Do you have experience in digital business card app? If so, What do you think?
  • What do you like or dislike about these app/paper business card? Tell me more.

Each with their problem and the way of use. Here are the summarized 2 type user quotes:

Physical card user :

"I always keep cards in my suits pocket when i got it. I haven't time save it"

"When i returned to the office, sort by name put into my cardcase, within 1 min."

"Sometimes i forget it, end up wasting time to find it on my cardcase"

"Sometimes i will jot down some client request and use Google Keep take notes after meeting"

"If i moved office or changed something, I need re-print card"

With experience in app :

"Organize them while i return on office after meeting, and around 3 min import on my mobile"

"I use app recently but I dislike complex step while i import the card, end up turn back to paper card."

"I used app in a short time, It’s so hard to operate, somewhat unpleasant not enough guide to done it"

Approach

Then, I determined what is major goal, and figure out what priorities of needs. Here are four point i suggested that way to guide myself to ideating framework where make design decsion.

#1

Through step-oriented make the task easier

Task flow is an essential part of the app. Re-ideating visually-fouced flow that helps for lessening users cognitive load, in which the users can focus on their tasks rather than on learning new things as major point. It also helps to heightens the motivation and anticipation for users using the app. In order to guide users operate easier, With light-hearted and concise headline in each step processing that able to lead them for further action decsion easily (e.g. "How would you like to design card", "What's my info" etc) so that reduce user frustration.

#2

Reducing complexity to avoid manually effort in the first place

Integrating task flow with OCR text recognition scanner, that helps to auto-fill extracted data into each corresponding contact info field. By doing this, able to avoid manual entry errors increasing success rate. This is great way to reduce the entry barrier of physical card user to going digial. Meanwhile, reserve the images after taken shot of paper card as a digital card appearance that able to skipped design card process if needed, which a way speed up time-on-task.

For the card visual:
Ready-made Template - which is shortcut way further to streamlining card visual flow, without any design skill.

Cloud-base Storage - which is feature given user reuse card template content to standardize with thier brand guideline avoid wasting time duplicating efforts.

#3

Shifting users behavior reduce extra time for card organize

Utilizing NFC featue that provides is automate exchange interaction, in which is fit well to motivate them to exchange card habit to reduce time afterwards to follow up.

Beside, providing new feature that allow user create the working schedules and reminders for the future things, make it following up easily in one place to avoid extra app usage occurs.

#4

Approach digital "Cardcase" improve usability

Establishing a Home as a "Digital Cardcase" concept like as real-life where as presented overview of card case. It’s also the easiest to understand in which make same logic as the organize thier physical card previously. The "Digial Card" is key visual element to improve whole app look and feel which compare with pure of contact list or somethat. Also, Placing the key task in one spot was access easily, and that fit one-hand operations simultaneously that ensure real-life usage and behavior.
