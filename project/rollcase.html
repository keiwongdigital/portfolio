roll:Case
A digital business card platform designed to organize business cards more efficiently in a stress-free, user-centric way to reduce the environmental impact of paper use.
Deliverable APP
Role UX - Research, Ideation, Wireframe, Usability Testing
UI - Visual Design, Prototyping

Overview

Due to COVID-19, there has been a shift towards everything digital and sustainability. However, I was inspired by an environmental news. Aiming to eliminate people's reliance on printing business cards, which is unnecessary and puts a huge strain on the earth's resources.

The aim is to adapt to user behaviour and reduce time on task for apps by optimising the overall digital experience, whether it's a paper card or current user. The highly vision was "PAPERLESS" to helping people be more eco-friendly; "Save the Planet"

Did you know ??

"Each year around 100 Billion business cards are produced worldwide, Has over 7 million trees are cut down to print paper business cards. 58% end up in the bin, and going to landfill."

Design Context

The Problem

Business cards are still a business courtesy in our society, but the fact that they are not eco-friendly. It is unlikely that we will see business cards fade out of business anytime soon.

Obviously, a large number of users still use paper business cards today. Whenever business cards are sorted, many of them end up in the rubbish bin causing pollution. On the other hand, the complexity of the tasks in the current app experience also creates barriers. This means that users will revert back to using paper business cards.

Design Challenge

"How might we reduce barriers and encourage paper card people going digital so that opportunity to achieve eco-friendly goal?"

"How might we design a stress-free task flow to well-organize business cards for current user more efficiency?"

Solution

Optimize step-oriented make the task smoothly that enable to lead them for action intuitively was key factor which helps for lessening users cognitive load. Plus, Provide more visually to operate that reduce time gap between step equally important, it's benfit to heightens the users motivation.

Paper Card Digitalize

The digitalize process was almost automatically not required input or design skill. These are extremely useful for people have huge paper card going to digital. Without frustrating them with too many working flow and high entry barriers so that more engaged paper users with.

swipe_up How it works

Step 1 :
Upon this demo, When user get a paper card, Tap on "Scan" button to launch in-app camera. Its recognizes each field form the screen via OCR scanner.

Step 2 :
Once take a card shot, Users can crop the card shot image.

Step 3 :
Extracted data will auto-fill to "Contact Import" screen what is field match with. Meanwhile, the card thumbnails appear on the top of the screen that allow users manually fix it or card re-shoting if there is any detail extracted incorrect. open_in_new Detail

Step 4 :
Once imported, "Keep the camera shot" was handy option which a shortcut way convert card shot image become as digital card apperance if user not required visual element. This way, helping users who sizable of paper cards to digitalize in the fast way. open_in_new Detail
