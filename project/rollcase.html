roll:Case
Even in digital era, and instant messaging very popular today, Business cards it still necessary tools today. roll:Case app gives users stress-free way to organize your business cards become more productive, and always prepared to exchange contact details no matter where you are.
Deliverables iOS APP
Scope UX - Strategy, Research, Ideation / UI - Visual Design, Wireframe, Prototyping

Project Goals

Establish a digitize business card platform that aims to user-centric approach to create a more effinient experience for people. In the meanwhile, to reduce the entry barrier to physical card user through a streamlined process simultaneously.

The highly vision was "PAPERLESS" to bring extra value on the project, "Save a tree"; "Save Mother Earth". Did you know? Each year 100 billion business cards are produced worldwide, 58% end up in the bin, and going to landfill.

Defining the Why?

Business cards are continues to be a business etiquette and social custom. As we’re unlikely to see business cards fade from the business landscape anytime soon.

While conducting early research, it's clear still having a huge user using paper business card today. A number of card ending up in the trash caused pollution whenever organzie thier business card. In terms of current experience. Those complex task that caused physical card users create barriers to entry, and also reflect current user unable to get motivate usage or back to paper card, because the current app is so inherent unfriendly.

Discovery and Empathize

Research

The first thing to do is to get to know the users through data. I started researches to better understand the goal. I conducted 15 interviewees which facebook and whatsapp group quick survey questions who was manage, gather business card with a client on a daily bases.

The following questions to learn about their current user facing:

  • How do you handle it when your get the business card from the client?
  • How do you currently organize your client business card? How much time spend on?
  • Do you have experience in digital business card app? If so, What do you think?
  • What do you like or dislike about these app/paper business card? Tell me more.

Each with their problem and the way of use. Here are the summarized 2 type user quotes:

Physical card user :

"I always keep cards in my suits pocket when i got it. I haven't time save it"

"When I returned to the office, sort by name put into my cardcase, within 1 min."

"I used app in a short time, It’s so hard to operate, card outlook part difficult to use, not enough guide how to done it"

"I don’t deal with that many business cards on a regular basis, Sometime dirt and moisture on card i found"

"If i moved office or changed something, I need re-print card"

With experience in app :

Pain Point & Insights

For discover findings what's the real problem. Here, I utilized card sorting to summarize user quote that narrowing down scope what are the most important takeaways.

#1PAIN POINT

Lack of time to organize

Insights :  Organizing the business cards are common pain point both of physical card and with experience in app users. They felt impatient and lack of time. Also, most of interviewees said who still using paper card. Due to long time run a business and store sizable stash of business cards, so they ending up waste time to organized.

#2PAIN POINT

Difficult operation

Insights :  The complex task that caused physical card users create barriers to entry and frustration, and also reflect current user unable to get motivate usage, which means the user will back to use paper card, because the task is so inherent unfriendly.

#3PAIN POINT

Digital card app not common

Insights :  Only 2/15 interviewees with experience in app, Most of peope only if provides clearly guidance of task flow will this interested in use. This also opens rooms for development for digital card app for potential users.

Framing the Strategy

Design Solution

Step-oriented make the task easier and smoothly

Task flow is an essential part of the app. Establishing a visually-fouced flow that helps for lessening users cognitive load and thier task completion intuitively, in which the users can focus on their tasks rather than on learning new things. It also helps to heightens the motivation for users using the digital card app as key point.

#1

Reduce task complexity, easier digitized

Creating a feature that reserve the images of paper card after taken shot as a digital card appearance, that able to skipped design card process. This is great way to reduce the entry barrier for physical card user going digial, and speed up time-on-task also.

In order to guide users operate easier, With light-hearted and concise headline in each step that able to lead them for further action decsion easily (e.g. "How would you like to design card", "What's my info" etc) so that reduce user frustration.

For the card visual:
Ready-made Template - which is shortcut way without any design skill further to streamlining card visual custom.

Cloud-base Storage - which is feature given stability for user reuse card template content to standardize with thier brand guideline avoid wasting time duplicating efforts.

#2

To shift card organize habit by NFC

Building a interchange process intergated with NFC feature. With this, Card infomation transfer instanly which fit well to motivate them to shifting card habit and physical card users engaging. This can helps to avoid data entry errors and better accuracy to eliminate afterwards card organize and follow up.

Plus, Offering new feature that allow user create the working schedules and reminders for the future things, make it following up easily in one place to avoid extra app usage occurs.

#3

Approach "Digital Cardcase" improve usability

Such a "Digital Cardcase" seem like real-life where presented cardcase appearance rather than only pure of contact list. It’s also the easiest to understand in which make interaction same logic as the physical card previously. Placing the key task in one spot was access easily, and that fit one-hand operations simultaneously that ensure real-life usage and behavior.

Physical Card Digitalize

These are extremely useful for people have huge paper card. Not required design skill for user perform a task easily.

Upon this demo, When user get a paper card, Tap on "Scan" button to launch in-app camera. Its recognizes each field form the screen via OCR scanner. After take the shot, Users can crop the card shot image. Once presss Tick button, the extracted data will auto-fill to "Contact Import" screen which field matched with. Meanwhile, the card thumbnails appear on the top of the screen that allow users manually fix it if there is any detail extracted incorrect.

Once imported, "Keep the camera shot" is handy option which is shortcut way convert card shot image become as digital card apperance, if user not required custom thier card visual. open_in_new Detail
