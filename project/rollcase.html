roll:Case
You may not be collecting business cards now like in years past? Right? roll:Case apps gives you easiest way to digitize your business cards. The digital card case are always with you, any time you'll need it. Whether it's from a face-to-face meeting or social event. Never fumble of business cards at a conference again. It important to helping you spend less time on manage.
Duration 3.5 Month
Scope iOS APP
Role UI Design / UX task

Goals

This new app aims to create digitize card platform. Implement new features and interactions to create a fresh and smoothly experience for business users. such as exchange, manage, create multiple business cards. Provide cloud base storage it possible for contact information to be shared with third-party tools like email, address books and CRMs. The high vision was paperless, "Save a tree", "Save Mother Earth". Did you know? 8 billion of cards we print each year end up in the bin within a week.

Role

During the project, I collaborated with develop team, I was responsible for bringing rollCase app to life from concept to high-fidelity prototypes. The process included extensive interviews, research, ideation, wireframes, UI design and usability testing.

Design Process

Define and Ideate

Nowadays, the people maintenance business cards inefficient, outdated and not eco-friendly. Receiving a physical business card is it can be easy to lose, The truth is, no one has the time to organize contacts and manually save business card details to their mobile. Also, we can not make a business card instantly.

Research

At the initial phase, I started quick researches to better understand the goal and user needs. We have interviewed 11 peoples who use physcial card and card app on a daily bases. Each with their pain point and the way of organize.

Here are the common issues faced:
→ "Easy to lose / Always keep cards in my suits pocket"
→ "Lack of time to organize / Sometimes I forget."
→ "if i just moved office or changed something? re-print card?"
→ "Exsiting card app UI ugly visual / Some process not clearly"

To better understand not only our end-to-end users, I researched other cards app their service structure, target users, and app/web product what problems exist. Through both of researches, we are clarified things that make up the core of the product that will appeal to the main target group.

Challenge

How can users feel easily and comfortable throughout manage their business card?

In short, to summarize in three stages solution we are focuses on;

  • Lower time costs
    Digital Card Exchange - Convenient way to exchange, organize and share thier business cards
  • Efficiently digitalize process
    Scaning Physical Card - Convert paper cards to digial version avoid manually
  • Simple interaction
    Create Own Digital Card - Intuitive user interface and attractive visual hierarchy

Design

Wireframe

After I got the revisions with teamates. I sketch out my ideas to better visualize the solution. After that, I started with wireframes phase, we focused on how to design a service that is more simple and intuitive experience. Visual

I decided three keywords for rollCase entire visual apperenance of the app: Realistic, Stylish and Professional.

The app is image-driven including a lot of digital cards and users avatars. Use dark background makes the digital cards visuals deeper and makes the content highly legible with the orange accent color. To improve user experience, usage huge set of icon can help users navigate and operate the app clearly and quickly. In the type color, I used white for the text and and included a great deal of grey description to give a calm and clean appearance.

Additionally, I hope take that for a good balance of usability and visual appeal. Design approaches varous engaging illustration elements to keep it fun while working.

Iterations

During testing the prototype phase, I fixed some accessibility issues. Also, simple A/B testing with interviewers where compare two versions of a app flow in order to optimize a certain result or metric and find out which of them is more effective especially paper card convert to digital card process. After that, I conducted prototype using XD which improved the interaction feedback and made the prototype feel more realistic.

Clearly structed and communicate effortlessly

Dashboard as a main screen is the core of the app. Key design element to be ‘Cards’ entire the app. Designing digital card I realized that there are many different types of cards. The challenge was to find the balance between different businesses' and card type, and be consistent with a uniform design line.

In Actually, Designing interaction as vertical scrolling which like real cardcase used the same notion here. The Bottom of navigation bars as core feature section, used frosted glass effect that incorporating static card and dynamic visuals, when scrolling where card are still blurred behind another, its more catchy. The quick filter panel allows a user to sort out they wanted, such as recent contact, favorites, company, group etc.

Provide textual prompts when input keywoards that offers you the options as soon as you are inputting query. Use filters to tune search aside from the history options. Tap on a card show more detail, contact info, social media, and people relationship on the contact page. Users can swipe left phone number icon make a phone call directly.

Eco-friendly way exchange contact, Easiest way to organize business cards

With NFC feature, enables the effortless and contactless exchange of data. It automated work almost completely meet users needs.

If the partner has rollCase app, even better. As video show, swipe up center" NFC exhange" icon in the dashboard. Users can simply swipe up your business card you wanted share. Once you’ve shared your card, they can save it directly to their phone. Also, share your card via QR code or vCard with the people even other person doesn’t the rollCase app.

From the Dashboard, long tap the digital card where lets user quickly share via email, WhatsApp, Facbook and Skype etc. it also comes with card manage setting if needed, such as edit and delete. Within the card detail page, where via mobile, add notes/schedule reminder with dates and group contacts.

Smart OCR helps save valuable time

With the OCR tech, it's helps users to digitize from paper card accurately and conveniently.

Launch the in-app camera and take a shot of physical card, it is a full camera scanning kit that has rectangle recognition, cropping and detection. Users just aim and tap to snap a photo Then, the result thumbnails of your scanned cards on the top of the screen. With OCR processing which converts information such as names, titles, company name into a editable format, and if there is any detail that's incorrect, user can manually fix it.

After that, the ready-made templates helps user as quickly and easily as possible. Once saved, the app creates a new card on the dashboard and automatically sync new contacts data to cloud database option also.

Fingertips create unique your brand

Provide intuitive create card UI is importent. This aim to user the easily design own unique cards without any design skill just minutes.

Within the create own card process, user can import contacts info via address book, mail and vCard sync option, so contact info is always up to date. Again, It prevent manual data entry errors.

In addition to ease of use, we looked for card design. A simple step by step UI for user built up the card visual which fully-customize such as adding head icon, logo, background image, template and advance layout option you want. So, the entire create digital card process was so simple.

Once saved as cloud storage where share with colleagues or teams and able to reuse card template content. Plus, the cards data was synchronize option where let users across multiple devices so that user can access contact info at anywhere, anytime.

Animated illustrations brings user interface to life

Impressive animation effects helps attract users attention and increases the level of trust in it.

With splash screen logo animation, it can reveal a app's idea, features, and making it instantly recognizable. Moreover, onboarding animated illustrations that show the app’s core information. Let user know the app does and the features it includes and more guidance. They’ll want to know what the app can do for them in addition to how to do it.
