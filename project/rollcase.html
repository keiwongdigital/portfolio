roll:Case
Even in digital era, business cards it still necessary tools today. roll:Case is a digital business card app that provides user-centric approach that gives users stress-free way to organize digital business cards become more efficient in a green way.
Deliverables iOS APP
Role UX - Research, Ideation, Wireframe, Usability Testing
UI - Visual Design, Prototyping

The Story

Due to COVID-19, there has been a shift towards everything digital and sustainability. However, I was inspired by a Eco-friendly news. Aim to remove the dependency people have on printing out business cards which is unnecessary, there was a huge strain on the resources of planet earth.

Adapt to the user’s behavior and narrow the gap of paper card users going digital through optimizing the overall experience. The highly vision was "PAPERLESS" to helping people be more eco-friendly; "Save the Earth"

Did you know ??

"Each year 100 Billion business cards are produced worldwide, Has over 7 million trees are cut down to print paper business cards. 58% end up in the bin, and going to landfill."

Overview of the Issue

Business cards are continues to be a business etiquette and social custom. We were unlikely to see business cards fade from the business landscape anytime soon.

It's clear still having a huge user using paper business card today. A number of card ending up in the trash whenever organzie thier business card, and which causes pollutions impact. However, In currently experienceing, The app task was complicated that make barriers either which of paper card users motivation going digital and current app user frustration also, which means the user will back to use paper card, which is why the digital card app is not widely accepted.

What's Design Challenge

"How might we design a stress-free and efficiency task flow to well-organize business cards for current user?"

In the meantime,

"How might we reduce barriers and encourage paper card people going digital so that opportunity to achieve eco-friendly goal?"

The first thing to do is to get to know the users through data. I started researches to better understand the goal and scope. I conducted 16 participants which facebook and whatsapp chat group though survey questions how to manage and gather business card on a daily bases.

Ideating the Strategy

Proposed Solution

Enhance step-oriented make the task smoothly

Aim to visually-fouced flow of which helps for lessening users cognitive load, and reducing time gap between step flow. So, the first focus was nailing user flow able to lead them for further action decsion easily. It also helps to heightens the motivation of both user types.

#1

Reduce card creation task complexity, easier digitized

Creating a feature that reserve the images of paper card after taken shot as a digital card appearance, that able to skipped design card step - ideal for both user types who want to have speed on task, and who unaware of design. This is great way which either for paper card user going digial easier, and speed up card creation time-on-task for app users also.

More feature for card visual creation :
Ready-made Template - Shortcut way without any design skill further to streamlining card visual creation.

Cloud Storage - A feature given user reuse card template to standardize with thier brand guideline avoid wasting time duplicating efforts.

#2

Shifting a card manage behavior by NFC

Building a interactive process that intergated with NFC feature. With this, card infomation interchange instanly and automatically in which helps to avoid much data entry and better accuracy. Such a way to motivate them to engage with app. This helps to eliminate card manage works and also are easy to share afterwards.

Plus, Offering new feature that allow user create the working schedules and reminders for the future things, make it following up easily in one place to avoid extra app usage occurs.

#3

"Digital Cardcase" approach improve usability

Designing home screen as "Digital Cardcase" where presented digital card hub appearance rather than pure of contact list. It’s also the easiest to understand of which make interaction logic as same as the paper cardcase. Plus, placing the core task button at one spot that fit for user one-hand operate for real-life usage and behavior.

Paper Card Digitalize

The digitalize process was almost automatically not required input or design skill. These are extremely useful for people have huge paper card to going digital. Without frustrating them with too many working flow or manually. The more highly engaged paper users are, the more likely they are reduce impact on the environment.

How it works

Upon this demo, When user get a paper card, Tap on "Scan" button to launch in-app camera. Its recognizes each field form the screen via OCR scanner. Once take a card shot, Users can crop the card shot image. then presss "Tick" button, the extracted data will auto-fill to "Contact Import" screen what is field match with. Meanwhile, the card thumbnails appear on the top of the screen that allow users manually fix it if there is any detail extracted incorrect.

Once imported, "Keep the camera shot" was handy option which a shortcut way convert card shot image become as digital card apperance if user not required visual element. This way, helping users who sizable of paper cards to digitalize in the fast way. open_in_new Detail
