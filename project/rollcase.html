roll:Case
Even in digital era, and instant messaging very popular today, Business cards it still necessary tools today. roll:Case app is digital business card app that gives users stress-free way to organize business cards become more productive, and always prepared to exchange contact details no matter where you are. The highly vision was "PAPERLESS" to bring extra value on the project; "Save the Earth" to helping people be more eco-friendly.
Deliverables iOS APP
Role UX - Research, Ideation, Usability Testing / UI - Wireframe, Visual Design, Prototyping

Project Goals

Establish a holistic of digitize business card platform that aims to user-centric approach to create a more efficient way organzie thier business card. Adapt to the user’s behavior and narrow the gap of paper card users going digital through optimizing the overall experience.

In this solo project, I was inspired and driven by Eco-news. Aim is to fully remove the dependency people have on printing out business cards which is unnecessary and a huge strain on the resources of planet earth.

Did you know ??

"Each year 100 Billion business cards are produced worldwide, 58% end up in the bin, and going to landfill."

Overview of the Issue

Business cards are continues to be a business etiquette and social custom. We were unlikely to see business cards fade from the business landscape anytime soon.

It's clear still having a huge user using paper business card today. A number of card ending up in the trash whenever organzie thier business card, and which causes pollutions impact. In currently experienceing, The app task was complicated that make barriers either which of paper card users motivation going digital and current app user frustration also, which means the user will back to use paper card, which is why the digital card app is not widely accepted.

Ideating the Strategy

The Solution

Enhance step-oriented make the task smoothly and visually

Task flow is an essential part of the app. Entire apps consist of three core feature: Card Creation, Card Exhange and Paper Card Digitalize. So, the first focus was nailing user flow and making it as simple and obvious as possible.

Aim to visually-fouced flow that helps for lessening users cognitive load and reduce time gap between step flow. To do so, the users can focus on their tasks completion intuitively. It also helps to heightens the motivation of both user types so that fits real-life usage.

With light-hearted and concise headline in each step that able to lead them for further action decsion easily (e.g. "What's my info", "Pick the Card Template Your Love" rather than "Import info etc), so that to eliminates stress with user who feels “Order to do to so”.

#1

Reduce task complexity, easier digitized

Creating a feature that reserve the images of paper card after taken shot as a digital card appearance, that able to skipped design card step - ideal for both user types who want to have speed on task and the unware of designing card visual. This is great way which either for paper card user going digial easier, and speed up time-on-task for app users also.

For card visual creation :
Ready-made Template - which is shortcut way without any design skill further to streamlining card visual creation.

Cloud Storage - which is feature given user reuse card template to standardize with thier brand guideline avoid wasting time duplicating efforts.

#2

Effortless way to shifting card manage behavior by NFC

Building a interactive process that intergated with NFC feature. With this, card infomation interchange instanly and automatically, that helps to avoid much data entry and better accuracy. Such a way to motivate them to engage with app, shift their mindset of card organize. This helps to eliminate afterwards card manage works and follow up.

Plus, Offering new feature that allow user create the working schedules and reminders for the future things, make it following up easily in one place to avoid extra app usage occurs.

#3

"Digital Cardcase" approach improve usability

Designing home screen as "Digital Cardcase" where presented digital card list view rather than pure of contact list. It’s also the easiest to understand in which make interaction same logic as the paper card previously. Placing the key task in one spot was access easily, and that fit one-hand operations simultaneously that ensure real-life usage and behavior.

Paper Card Digitalize

A challenge here is I wanted the interaction to feel intuitively. Not required design skill for digitalize task easily. These are extremely useful for people have huge paper card. Without frustrating them with too many working flow or manually. The more highly engaged paper users are, the more likely they are reduce impact on the environment.

Upon this demo, When user get a paper card, Tap on "Scan" button to launch in-app camera. Its recognizes each field form the screen via OCR scanner. After take the shot, Users can crop the card shot image. Once presss Tick button, the extracted data will auto-fill to "Contact Import" screen what is field match with. Meanwhile, the card thumbnails appear on the top of the screen that allow users manually fix it if there is any detail extracted incorrect.

Once imported, "Keep the camera shot" is handy option which is shortcut way convert card shot image become as digital card apperance, if user not required custom thier card visual. This way, ensure that the user has already been able to satisfy thier needs on the simple way. open_in_new Detail
