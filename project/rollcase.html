roll:Case
You may not be collecting business cards now like in years past? Right? roll:Case gives users eco-friendly way to digitize your business cards. The digital card case are always with you any time, whether it's from a face-to-meeting or social event.
Deliverables iOS APP
Scope UX - Strategy, Research, Ideation / UI - Visual Design, Wireframe, Prototyping

Project Goals

Establish a digitize card platform that aims to user-centric approach to create a seamlessly experience for business-man. Reduce the entry barrier to physical card user through a streamlined process simultaneously so that increasing the potential market. The highly vision of the project was PAPERLESS, "Save a tree"; "Save Mother Earth". Did you know? Each year 100 billion business cards are produced worldwide, 58% end up in the bin, and going to landfill.

DISCOVERY

Research

The first thing to do is to get to know the users through data. I started researches to better understand the goal. I conducted 10 interviewees which Facebook group vote poll, whatsApp group questionnaire who use physcial card and digital card app on a daily bases. Each with their frustration and the way of use. Here are the summarized user quotes.

Physical card user :

"I always keep cards in my suits pocket after meeting, Sometimes Im not haven't time to save the card, End up wasting time to find it"

"If i moved office or changed something, I need re-print card"

"I used app in a short time, not enough hints how to done it"

Digital card user :

"I use app recently but I dislike complex step while i import the card, It was hard to operate"

"Existing app was limited, sometimes i use Google Keep or paper droping notes"

“I can’t manage my card easily."

"Can't share it to my workmate after template created"

Synthesising Finding

Here, I summarized user quote that narrowing down scope what are the real problem, Here are three pain point :

#1PAIN POINT

Lack of time to organize and feel extra work

Organizing the business cards are common pain point both of digital or physical card users. Sometimes feel impatient and extra work they ended up waste time to finding the card. Also, most of interviewees said who still using phyiscal card. Due to long time run a business and store sizable stash of business cards that need to get organized.

#2PAIN POINT

Difficult operation which make user frustration

In term of App usability, interviewees mentioned bad experience they used. Feel frustrated on complex step and without clear guide in action performing their tasks, and not meet real-life scenarios usage as well.

#3PAIN POINT

Not enough feature accomplish task

Interviewees expressed need extra app usage, current app feature does not fulfill thier task and expectation.

Empathize with users facing

Thinking Point :  How they currently business card management?

The truth is, people always looking for saving time in daily life, most people haven't spend time to organize contacts after meeting, and manually save business card details to their mobile or put into cardcase. It's easy to forget or sometimes lose it.

Thinking Point :  Digital card app was not common in Hong Kong

It's clear still having a huge user using paper business card today. The complex operation caused physical card users create barriers to entry, unable to get motivate them usage also. This opens plenty of rooms for development for digital card app for potential users.

Thinking Point :  They dont worries influence of environmental pollution!

A number of card ending up in the trash whenever organzie thier business card. Besides, people can not make a business card instantly if someone’s information changed they had to order an entirely new stack of business cards. There's also extra pollution whenever the people lose the card and production of the card.

IDEATION

Strategy

Then, I determined what is major goal, and figure out what priorities of needs. Here are four point i suggested that way to guide myself to ideating framework.

#1

Through step-base flow make the task easier

Re-ideating more visual way of step-base solution that able to lessening users cognitive load. Meanwhile, By providing ready-made template without any design skill that speed up the process. With light-hearted headline in each step processing that able to lead them for further action decsion easily (e.g. "How would you like to design card", "What's my info").

#2

Eliminate manually effort to reduce physical card user entry barrier

By in-App OCR scanner processing, that auto-fill text field and editable format after extract data. By doing this, able to avoid manual entry errors so that reduce user frustration. Additionaly, reserve the images after taken card shot as a digital card appearance that able to skipped design card process if users unnecessary. This great way to reduce the entry barrier of physical card user to going digial which almost fully automated actions. Offer cloud-base template for corporate user so that user can reuse card template content to standardize with thier brand guideline avoid wasting time duplicating efforts.

#3

Shift organizing business card behavior

Throught NFC featue exchange which is almost completely automated, which a well-fitted after meeting to motivate them to shifting organize business card habits so that reduce extra work and time afterwards.

Once a card exchanged, that allow user create the working schedules and reminders for the future things, make it following up easily avoid extra app usage.

#4

Approach digital "Cardcase" improve usability

Make same logic as the organize thier business card they previously used. Establishing a Home as a "Digital Cardcase" like as real-life where as overview of card case. This is great way to showing key element of "CARD" compare with pure of contact list. Meanwhile, all key task in one spot was access easily, and that fit one-hand operations simultaneously that ensure real-life habits and interactions.

IDENTITY

Initial Designing task flow and Feedback

I reviewed necessary features and focused on interaction that increase task completion rate. i drafted 3 key paper mock-up for rapid tesing that are essential to the concept. I tested with two participants that help me understand the user and how they interacting with the app.

1. Home Interaction

Here, I tried to avoid the unapplicable design based on real life scenarios, and keep it one-hand operate smoothly also. They love the core feature available with one places, and digital card with vertical scroll interaction, they expressed "Card ease access", "Feature buttons easily to use". Perhaps, heightens the motivation and anticipation for users using the app.

Iterating feedback :
In order to fit well people finger, I removed describe text of icon in mid filter bar shown icon symbol only. Put the card list a bit upward.

2. Own card creation

Participants able to accomplish tasks the first time and noted pretty good results too. That able to lead them for further action easily. Also, I observe that the customizable option was most used from participants than build-in template.

Iterating feedback :
They said completion step a bit longer, So I streamlined the step from four to three step and some interaction element issues.

3. Import physical card

In terms of the scan card, They were unsure correct data of the card shot was filled if unextracted data. They missing some contact data beneath it i noted.

Iterating feedback :
A card preview of the screen was added accordingly that help them for editing if data extract not correctly.
