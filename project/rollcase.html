roll:Case
Do you have stacks of business cards sitting around your home or office? Want to finally get those cards organized and well control? A roll:Case gives users stress-free way to digitize your business cards. The digital cardcase are always with you any time and up-to-date, whether it's from a face-to-meeting or social event.
Deliverables iOS APP
Scope UX - Strategy, Research, Ideation / UI - Visual Design, Wireframe, Prototyping

Project Goals

Establish a digitize card platform that aims to user-centric approach to create a more effinient experience for business-man. In the meanwhile, to reduce the entry barrier to physical card user through a streamlined process simultaneously so that increasing the potential market.

The highly vision was "PAPERLESS" to bring extra value on the project, "Save a tree"; "Save Mother Earth". Did you know? Each year 100 billion business cards are produced worldwide, 58% end up in the bin, and going to landfill.

DISCOVERY

Research

The first thing to do is to get to know the users through data. I started researches to better understand the goal. I conducted 10 interviewees which facebook and whatsapp group quick survey questions who was manage, gather business card with a client on a daily bases.

The following questions to learn about their current user facing:

  • How do you handle it when your get the business card from the client?
  • How do you currently organize your client business card? How much time spend on?
  • Do you have experience in digital business card app? If so, What do you think?
  • What do you like or dislike about these app/paper business card? Tell me more.

Each with their problem and the way of use. Here are the summarized 2 type user quotes:

Physical card user :

"I always keep cards in my suits pocket when i got it. I haven't time save it"

"When i returned to the office, sort by name put into my cardcase, within 1 min."

"Sometimes i forget it, end up wasting time to find it on my cardcase"

"Sometimes i will jot down some client request and use Google Keep take notes after meeting"

"If i moved office or changed something, I need re-print card"

With experience in app :

"Organize them while i return on office after meeting, around 2 min import on my mobile"

"I use app recently but I dislike complex step while i import the card, not enough guide to done it"

"I used app in a short time, It’s so hard to operate"

"I wish I was share it to my workmate after i created"

IDEATION

Strategy

Then, I determined what is major goal, and figure out what priorities of needs. Here are four point i suggested that way to guide myself to ideating framework.

#1

Through step-base flow make the task easier

Task flow is an essential part of the app. Re-ideating visually-oriented flow that able to lessening users cognitive load in which the users can focus on their tasks rather than on learning new things as major point.

Meanwhile, By providing ready-made template without any design skill so that speed up the time on task. With light-hearted headline in each step processing that able to lead them for further action decsion easily (e.g. "How would you like to design card", "What's my info" etc).

#2

Reducing complexity to avoid manually effort in the first place

By in-App OCR scanner processing, that auto-fill text field and editable format after extract data. By doing this, able to avoid manual entry errors so that reduce user frustration. This is great way to reduce the entry barrier of physical card user to going digial in which almost avoid users manually actions.

Additionaly, reserve the images after taken card shot as a digital card appearance that able to skipped design card process if users unnecessary, which increased success rate in the user task.

Offer new feature of cloud-base template storage, so that user can reuse card template content to standardize with thier brand guideline avoid wasting time duplicating efforts.

#3

Shifting users behavior reduce extra card organize and time afterwards

Integrating NFC featue that provides is completely automated exchange flow, which a well-fitted to motivate them to exchange card behavior reduce afterwards card organizing.

Additionaly, once user card exchanged that provides new feature that allow user create the working schedules and reminders for the future things, make it following up easily in one place to avoid extra app usage.

#4

Approach digital "Cardcase" improve usability

Make same logic as the organize thier business card they previously used. Establishing a Home as a "Digital Cardcase" concept like as real-life where as overview of card case. This is great way to showing key element of "CARD" compare with pure of contact list. All the key task in one spot was access easily, and that fit one-hand operations simultaneously that ensure real-life behavior and interactions.
