和酌 Japanese Sake Campaign
"和酌" is an e-commerce platform that offers a variety of japan wine-related experiences and a new lifestyle. Aim to sell mainly fresh sake at the same price in Japan and classic snack that go well with its sake. "傳遞純粹" is the unchanging core value of "和酌", representing the natural of respect that surround all sake lover.
Deliverable Social Media Campaign
Role Visual Design, Order Form Design

The Project

The main objective of the project was to create an effective and engaging digital marketing campaign to increase sales of the online shop and a selection of different regional sake brand events. The campaign was designed to allow the target group to experience a thorough "地酒" element journey exploring the unique local sake regions brand, which seamlessly guiding customers to make purchases on the website to build intrigue and demand for new services.

Ideating the Design

Design Strategy

The campaign's visual language reinforces its traditional sake brewing while still reflecting its contents to promote sake monthly delivery service. The engaging content is key to stop those scrolling thumbs in their tracks.

Sake has had a close relationship with the natural environment of Japanese people since ancient times. For this reason, I present the hand sketch 4 raw essential materials that passion for natural, sake brewery respect for the land. That represents the deep connection to the land, hard work, and authenticity of the sake. A journey of discovery and respect for terroir; making use of "酒造" background image, characteristic, combined with minimalist style.
