和酌 Local Sake Campaign
"和酌" is an e-commerce platform that offers a variety of japan wine-related experiences and a new lifestyle. Aim to sell mainly fresh sake at the same price in Japan and classic snack that go well with its sake.
Deliverable Promotional Campaign
Role Visual Design, Order Form Design

The Project

The main objective of the project was to create an effective and engaging sake monthly delivery campaign to increase sales of the online shop and a selection of different regional sake brand events. The campaign was designed to allow the target group to experience a thorough "地酒" element journey exploring the unique three local sake regions brand, which seamlessly guiding customers to make purchases on the website to build intrigue and demand for new services.

Campaign Touchpoint

The main target audience was somewhere between sake beginners and slightly more experienced tasters who were at home on weekends with friends and especially at parties for house parties. Social media platforms and customer subscriber databases (EDMs) were used as the first touchpoints, giving the customer sake details that they needed to know before purchasing goes perfectly with their mission and business.

Defining what's Design Framework

What's Design Challenge

"Communicate to sake beginners and slightly more experienced tasters alike the richness of its inherent appeal and the best time to taste it at home or at a party, making it easier for them to make a buying decision."

Ideating the Campaign Goals

Design Behind the Idea

Sake has had a close relationship with the natural environment of Japanese people since ancient times. Likewise,"傳遞純粹" is the unchanging core value of "和酌", representing the natural respect that surrounds all sake lovers. I roughed out the local sake production in desk research. This campaign communicated the local sake-brewing through storytelling that embodies the essence of traditional Japanese craftsmanship. I created "地酒 育成純粹" as a headline that is in line with the brand slogan to engage audiences, which is key message to keeping them scrolling in their tracks.

Defining the Visual Language

Campaign Message

Designed campaign headline that presence of contrast between natural and geometric. The landforms with dynamic lines for the three local sake regions—Nagano, Yamagata, and Aomori—are the key visuals to express the passion for natural Japanese sake and the brand's respect for its own land.

The entire theme uses only a different scale of deep gray and white that creates the right associations with the product through a minimalist and clean language. The typeface is a Fangsong font with strokes that have thick and thin variations that reflect content lightly.

Ideating the Visual Communication

Enrich the Campaign Story through Sake Brewing

To further convey this message, the visual identity was designed to emphasise the brewing key processes of “Rice Polishing”, "Koji Making" and “Fermentation” to reach target audience. Those elements convey the sake brand's deep connection to the land, Technique, Hard work and Authenticity. Using the descriptive background images and features of "酒造" combined with the raw style of the sketches and pattern, it shows a journey of discovery and respect for the terroir. These are sakes that somehow are alive.

Specifiy Purchase Forms Design

Designed specific order forms which making readable, consistent and indicate through text and visually distinguished as possible, and embed it into client website. The goal here is to visit website purchase by social media ads and EDMs.
