Hong Kong Gold Coast Hotel Website Redesign
Gold Coast Hong Kong is part of the SINO Group. We were tasked to create an immersive website that has visual connections to the corporate brand. At the same time the website also had to have its own design language and convenient understandable to the average user to communicate the offerings of the SINO Group.
Deliverables Website
Role UI - Ideating Design, Visual, Prototyping

The Project

About Hotel

Gold Coast Hong Kong sit on Tuen Mun Coast celebrated coast. Exclusively private seaview and a resort-like ambience. The hotel with 453 hotel guest rooms and suites, including 10 kid-themed rooms and superb panoramic marina sea views. The hotel promises days of exhilarating leisure and night of relexed, indulgent comfort. The hotels attracting local holiday-goers and international, as well as friendly neighbourhood communities attracted to the outdoors, sun, sand and combination of great facilities and activities designed with kids.

Business Objectives

- Increase family booking service conversion rate
- Generate new leads or reach-outs
- Better align brand offerings with demand

Target Audience

The primary target audience segment was focused on local familys in the age group of 25–50, who fancy a break from their home for the weekend that make this a particularly good family staycation option.

Defining what's Design Opportunities

What's Design Challenge

The SINO Hotel Group would like to further increase their occupancy figures. Besides marketing measures, they decide to improve the website booking process. We were exploring end-to-end user journey in hotel-booking process and revamping the entire flow, structure, and design. while also attempting to provide a significant business sided boost.

As a corporation that existed for decades, we were careful to creating authentic brand experiences that reinforce its group objectives for existing customers and the hotel's image. The main focus was on promote brand loyalty by pleasing the hotel experience for guests.

Design Strategy

Designed to convey the message of ease and SINO Hotel Group. To improve the booking experience based on the hotels' best-selling products and family packages. Allow thier customers to maximize the use of our products while striving for business growth. The Hotel tagline, “Nature. Leisure. Pleasure.” comes directly from SINO group hotel, it needs to communicate with its target audience in an approachable way. Through SINO brand principles and constraints, we created a visual language to enhance their form of hotel communication.

Ideating the Design

Discover

The core process was booking. First step is to know more about the hotel and the users we want to reach, We needed to get an overview of how user book rooms on hotel sites. As I had no specialist knowledge of hotel booking processes, I run usability tests for a deeper look at the best practice and patterns of the booking process thorough research to several hotels. Driven to create an empathic application that helped people with an task.

User Flow

We conducted the feature prioritisation that enough features and touch point to satisfy the brief as well as the target user needs. in order to specify the actions users would be able to perform and start sketching the user flows.

Add-ons Services Task Flow

We understand that hotels also provide a wide-range services for family trip other than just selling rooms. We also have to include not only the users booking experience, but also the hotel business.

The most important challenge in adding this feature is finding the right spot. Because the feature is just subsidiary and not mandatory, we need to put it on the phase where the user feels the most comfortable with not too annoying but still serve its initial purpose: to sell. We grouping these services into three add-ons groups: Meals, Room Complements and Activities. This shows the necessary successive interactions of the user from the search to the completion of the booking.

This shows the necessary continuous interaction of the user from the initial operation to the completion of the booking with add-ons services:

We decided to put the phase after the user chooses the hotel and before the payment process. Since this step is not mandatory, users can opt to not click it and go straight to the payment process. With abandoned bookings being so prevalent in the process it’s important to limit this as much as possible.

Sketches out Design Concept

Once we have the client content in place and brand identity, we can important to organize it to make readable pages. The initial idea was to build on the content richness and separate the different areas of information nicely to improve visual clarity and keep things light. It's important to figure out the client's brand identity before we start designing to make sure we're not compromised or limited in our design.

Crafting the Design Element

Design Language

My goal was to create a cozy atmosphere while conveying a bright and lively visual. I created the hotel's own design language based on brand constraints, while making it easy for the average family group to understand and experience the hotel as a place of leisure and comfort. Also, Keep visual vibrant of a page layout should match to hotel's objectives.

Grid System

I create a scalable grid framework that is clean, clarifies information hierarchy and that can be utilized for the future.

Design System

The color scheme derives directly either form the SINO Group, visual consistency and a strong brand identity. The primary color captures the brand's energy, while the different of saturations gold color adds balance. By integrating the branding colors into the entire design, we create cohesive user experience that reinforces the thier values.

Font were derived from the styleguide, and selected upon the SINO group design system. We considered all possible visual and textual information about hotel rooms. Highlight whatever content should grasp customer’s attention using icon and different weight text. The CTA button design was the revised pattern based on client library element. The most important factors had to be most prominent. Iconography is aim to explanation of room types and bed sizes and how they differ from each other.

Implementating the Key Solution

Booking Process

Redesign the booking interaction to give the website a more dynamic layout, yet effective and rid of any clunky user experiences which reducing the effort needed to get to the final booking page and separating the different information areas increases visual clarity. That should be intuitive for the user and divided into three steps. With process bar illustrate which step the user is in. Online bookers need just the right amount of information to make that booking, not too little and definitely not too much.

Booking CTA button is one of the most critical elements. This button should be prominently displayed on the process for further process should be clearly visible. The button will immediately navigate the potential guest through to hotel booking engine where they can easily complete their reservation within a few clicks.

Add-ons Services sub-step

After brainstorming potential project risks with the team. We add sub steps that will enhance user experience in buying add ons. Users can add the quantity of the add-ons that you would like to buy, plus adding notes or special request to it. Then, if you want to know more about it, you can simply click on the the images or "About" button, to see the detail. Adjusting quantity will be a matter of clicks, and to create a seamless and familiar experience, so users would naturally follow their instinct.

Placement and arrangement of add-ons must be considered. Timing of pop-up should be chosen so that the user is not disturbed in the selection or reading process.
