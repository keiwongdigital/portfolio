Design Context

Overview

Develop an Themed-Bookstore experience that centred around Jin-Yong's novels ambience. Aimed to user-centred approached that was fulfilling novels lover reading habit, and re-building relationships between martial arts novels and audiences deeply.

This was an individual project started base on my experience and design challenge. His novels helped people inspire their fantasies in mind, including me. So I’m going to share the process I went through MVP methodology to focus on key deliverables.

The Problem

Existing Jin Yong's novels reader app were not portraying value and long-term reading behaviour to their audiences

Dr. Jin Yong's is legends of martial arts novel. His works is fantasy and iconic. Most readers expressed their love for his works. Typically, People reads a novel takes long times. However, people nowdays it's not easy to make time for reading. Those readers has "Enjoy" novels extremly and spend over 2 hour average. Beside, they needs frequently action of scrolling swipe down or left during the reading on mobile screen, in which caused unsmoothly reads journey also.

What's Design Challenge

"How might we make Jin-Yong's novels readers to be controllable in a way to keep track of time that they’re read flexible, while conveying the reading experience more comfortably and valuable??"

The Solution

Transform e-book reader into Jin-Yong's Theme-Bookstore enhance entire experiences rather than pure of text e-reader. Tablet first approach that giving novels readers comfortably even a ton of words novels bring the user comfort as well, where differentiate from competitors in the market.

Research and Empathize

Knowing the Novel Readers

Kicking off, I started my research by understanding the problem I’m trying to solve. I planned a quick study with 8 people who reads novel within the target audience to gather diverse perspectives, all of whom had experienced before and try to get their thoughts. Asked them questions via Facebook Jin-Yong's novels fans group voting:

  • Which platform do they use for reading Jin-Yong's novel? Why hard-copy? Why e-Reader app?
  • How much time do they dedicate to reading, How often do they finish? How many times do they read a novel?
  • What are the time do they generally read?
  • Is reading at home or public area mostly?
  • Any issue faced when reading novels on current platforms (hard-copy/app)?

Plus, I assigned some simple tasks to 2 people to see how they would personally use it and identify reading issue. There are:

#1  Feasible - in-App Reading
#2  Feature - Check Reading status and History
#3  Behaviour - Time Spending

Analysing the Competitors

I tested 3 competitior on the market which gave me current app patterns and which feature have. Majority of competitior was lack of a detailed information about the reads activities. Beside, the chapters illustration are missing which is crucial of martial arts novels structural of integrity.

Ideating the Design Strategy

Adopting a hypothesis to driven the design

Below, I proceed by framing two hypothesis based on research previously. That can serve as the basic throughout my design strategy. With this, can be further explored solutions through well-defined approach.

Hypothesis #1

Every novel readers needs a exact status reading

WHY ?

Most people who trend to multi-task nowadays, it's reasonable to assum that readers would want to do other activities like playing games or somewhat on social media while reading. Just a page number bookmarking option can be demotivating thier reading efficiency. So, They valued a tracable status and detail what is reads on. Proposed Solution

Transform your ebook reader into a Jin Yong themed bookstore for an all-encompassing experience

I transitioned about the product strategy to built specific to a tablet platform. Through reimagine the theme-bookstore rather than pure of text e-reader. This approach is that giving novels readers multiple levels of imagery and valuable, where differentiate from competitors in the market.

timer

Enhance activitie for long-form reads

Providing more informative of novels activities that include Current percentage of reads, Evaluate the finish time of novel and Detail info of last reading.

Apply suitable color scheme for long period reads that provides the best legibility to reduce eye-stress with text. During the research phase previoulsy, there's no such app that is providing this option for users.

add_reaction

Connect novel reader to build closer relationships

Having categories of about the Jin-Yong's were, such as Jin Studies research (金學) & martial arts relevant book at the Theme-bookstore that enhance readers experience beyond just novels and expand readers group.

Utilizing novels visual element arcoss the whole UI to enhance ambience of Jin-Yong-themed bookstore, especially presented in Jin-Yong's works was important.

Creating value-added content that about the well-known characters and martial arts which allow user interact with. Make it more memorable experience.

Crafting the Design

Design System & Visual Language

Whole stage belongs to Jin-Yong's novels element. Here, I outlined 2 keywords: "Originality" and "Iconic". These are necessary elements of the novels, which ideating to design App logo also.

Since the pages are going to be filled with many book covers, it is ideal to keep the app colors as minimal as possible. Using light bit yellow as background colors like printed versions, Wine red as accent colors as Jin-Yong's seal color. Besides, I've also customize four set of book mockup and a unique vector calligraphy element as well.

Digital Illustration

Personally, I am impressed with the infinite possibilities that Jin-Yong’s novels offer. So, I did spend time thinking about the visual. I decided to create something which idea from the novels and imbue them with my own feelings. For this purpose, I created martial arts and storyline of novels illustration as innovative visual elements and help users feel more connected.
