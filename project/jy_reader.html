Jin-Yong's Novels Themed-bookstore
Are you a martial arts novels avid? If yes, probably you are no stranger to Jin Yong.
Jin-Yong's is the pen name of Dr Cha Leung-yung, world bestselling martial arts novelist. His works elevated the popular martial arts novel to an artistic level. Were deeply influencing the development of chinese-speaking world over the past decades, which inspired film, television, comic book and video game adaptations.

Most people said: "凡有華人的地方，就有人認識金庸".
Deliverables Tablet App
Scope UX - Strategy, Research, Ideation / UI - Visual Design, Prototyping

Project Goals

Re-create an Themed-Bookstore experience that centred around Jin-Yong's novels ambience. instead of pure of e-Reader app. Aimed to user-centred approached that fulfilling their hobby in novels reading, and building relationships between martial arts novels and audiences.

This was an individual passion project based on my own experience. Though I am not in the digital publishing field, I did however like the idea of re-designing Jin-Yong novels e-Reader. I take full of the roles involved in designing a product UX and UI.

DISCOVERY

Research

Kicking off, I did an app analyzes and understand in the market which include usability and features. Then, in order to validate these observations, and try to get their thoughts. I conducted testings about the app with 10 people (who reads digital version and printed version) using Facebook group vote poll. Plus, I assigned some simple tasks to see how they would personally use it and reading behavior patterns.

Task 1 :  Navigating entire App
Task 2 :  Check reading status and History
Task 3 :  Time spending
Task 4 :  Reading on difference scenes: Home / Public transit

Next, I through card sorting to attempting to further group the problem by user quotes. Some pain points were found:

#1PAIN POINT

Limited Reading Status

All of them mentioned that haven't a exact reading details of novels thier reads. Only page bookmark seem pretty basic, which caused inconvenience for long period reading users especially. With 2/10 mentioned the some function error or crash also.

#2PAIN POINT

Uncomfortable Reading Platform

With 6/10 people mentioned need long scrolling and swiping on the screen frequently. Mobile screen is not ideal for novels reading. Also, they reads at MTR has interference and eye strain. 1 people expressed reads on Amazon Kindle with well-experienced better than mobile.

#3PAIN POINT

Lack of Book genres

With 3/10 of them said not having Jin-Yong's novels relevant book they interested. Existing app haven't fulfill users needs.

#4PAIN POINT

Unattractive Interface

With 5/10 of them expressed the app "Ugly, Too plain" especially avid readers. They felt that the design of reader felt too “Boring” "Seem like reading article"; they wanted the app to feel uplifting.

Empathize with users facing

Thinking Point :  Every readers wanted a detail status of reading

Typically, reading a novel takes long times, nowday it's not easy to make time for reading. They tend to want to know more about the reading status before actually reading it. Seeing only a bookmarked page number can be demotivating and lower thier reading efficiency and motivation also. Thus, a detailed and traceable reading status more importantly. This help the users meets thier reading behaviour or multitask audiences especially.

Thinking Point :  Long period on mobile reading caused uncomfortable

Some readers has "Enjoy" novels extremly and spend over 3 hour average. They needs frequently action of scrolling swipe down or swipe left during reading process. Hence, they looking for better devices and places to read more fluently. So, they ended up trend to printed version or amazon kindle version. That's why the users perfer to home reading novels.

Thinking Point :  Chance of lose the potential audience

They perfered to discover novels relevant book after they reads novels. Current app haven't offer relevant selection. Thus, they endup to purchase either other e-book store or physical version.

Thinking Point :  Overall look and feel that can not get users interested

Jin Yong's is legends of martial arts novel. His works is fantasy and iconic. Some of readers expressed their love for his works. Text only version and lack of novels element had made thier disappointed.

IDEATION

Solution

#1  Developing the Themed-Bookstore

Transform the e-book reader app came up to a Jin-Yong's theme bookstore, where through reconstructe the whole experience and giving novels readers multiple levels of imagery and feelings to gain more value as well.

  • Building a bookshelf, that provides book selections which in novels research or martial arts relevant book, Jin Studies (金學) etc.
  • Creating novels illustrations that able to increaes ambience of Jin-Yong themed-bookstore. Additionaly, offering interactive value-added content that about the well-known characters and martial arts which are main element of the Jin-Yong novels.

Rationale : During the user research phase, I identified that the approach of themed bookstore was aligning well with the acheive users goals concurrently. These fresh approaches to expand target audience also.

#2  Reinforces reading status more informative

Providing a traceable novels reading status, such as history what current reading, and evaluate time for next reading. By showing percentage that ease way understand rather than page number.

Rationale : Detail tracking that able to increase reading efficiency and motivation. Makes it even the thickest of novels more friendly; This is especially to reduce user stress, and meets thier reading behaviour.

#3  Reading Platform Thinking

Designing a "Tablet-first" approach where landscape screen almost same as physical hard-copy size. It is crucial to make them feel comfortable to reading in wide screen. Below are the picked data that supports the "Tablet-first" methodology:

Also, I wish to apply suitable color scheme for long period reading to reduce body text visual stress and the best legibility.

DESIGN

User Flow

As I previously mentioned, which is establishing of a themed-bookstore not being redesign of ebook reader, so i created new a user flow of how the designing tasks would connect with each other. Walk me through with a roadmap to base design solutions.

Sketch Low-Fi Wireframes

I started key idea sketching about 4 screens with the user flows in minds on a paper prototype. I don’t spend any time thinking too much about the Hi-fi wireframes. Hence, The whole layout of elements and the details can be created in the interface stage with more time.

Design System

Visual whole stage belongs to Jin-Yong's novels element. I outlined 2 keywords: "Originality" and "Iconic". These are necessary elements of the novels, which ideating to design App logo also.

The overall UI design is simple and emotional to establish a connection. Since the pages are going to be filled with many book covers, it is ideal to keep the app colors as minimal as possible. A light bit yellow as background colors while wine red as accent colors. Besides, I've also customize four set of book mockup and a unique vector calligraphy element as well. It make the message and interactions clealy.

Digital Illustration

Personally, I am impressed with the infinite possibilities that Jin-Yong’s novels offer. So, I did spend time thinking too much about the visual element. I decided to create something which idea from the novels and imbue them with my own feelings. For this purpose, i created martial arts and storyline novels illustration as innovative visual elements and consistent with the whole UI.

A Fresh viewpoint on Themed-Bookstore

This approach was completely providing a new landing experience. With dynamic infinite-loop posterboard navigation, I deliberately martial arts illustration as entry page to relate to their respective section, which allows to keep people interested through the entire navigation that evoke emotions immediately and make it more memorable experience.

In "Bookstore" page, which sperated into the three type: 《細讀巨匠》 Jin-Yong’s Novels collections, 《江湖以外》 Novels reference book and《論盡金學》 Jin Studies research book as horizontal slider presented. A rest of the page is reserved for specific offer info and popular novels banner etc. collections Bookstore

Reinforced in-App Reading experience

In "My Bookshelf" page can keep track of reading status easily that contain of currently, percentage of pages reads, last reading time and chapter/pages detail. No longer need to assume how much time it will take to reading. The app will evaluate a novels that take time for next time reading. This would be shown to users every time they reads done and update current reading progress instanly. Also, shown a history of reading list with progress bar. collections My Bookshelf

User able to open user preference setting panel by top right button. the quick swiping button with page info what a current page shown when users swiping. Moreover, There are variety of option which is customize option to suit users reading behavior, such as font face, size, colors, night mode, page turn interactions and bookmarks with auto-saving the currently etc. collections User preference panel

As prototyping shown, the Tablet-first approach was smoothly and comfortable reading with wide screen, which is feel less cramped as well as better for novels reading. The novels illustrations between the chapter was well presented that keep feel "Real" same as printed version. collections Reader

Suitable color scheme help for eye strain

The "Eye protect mode" as new color scheme with a specific for reading long period. A background color value as #f4f4ec and gray font as #333, with a bit emboss paper texture same as printed version. This combination of color scheme was give out a soft and natural which has less of a stimulating effect on our eyes. That provides natural contrast and readability for body text.

Credit: The color scheme according to "Luz Rello - Bigham Human-Computer Interaction Institute & Carnegie Mellon University"
