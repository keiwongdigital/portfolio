Jin-Yong's Novels Themed-bookstore
Are you a martial arts novels avid? If yes, probably you are no stranger to Jin Yong.
Jin-Yong's is the pen name of Dr Cha Leung-yung, world bestselling martial arts novelist. His 15 works elevated the popular martial arts novel to an artistic level, and even generating a sort of common language and iconic.

Most people said:
"凡有華人的地方，就有人認識金庸".
Deliverables Tablet App
Scope UX - Strategy, Research, Ideation / UI - Visual Design, Prototyping

Project Goals

Develop an Themed-Bookstore experience that centred around Jin-Yong's novels ambience. Aimed to user-centred approached that was fulfilling novels lover reading habit, and building relationships between martial arts novels and audiences deeply.

This was an individual project started from my design challenge and own experience. His novels helped people inspire their fantasies in mind, including me. So I’m going to share the process I went through MVP methodology to focus on key deliverables.

Overview of the Issue

Existing novels reader app were not portraying value and experience

Dr. Jin Yong's is legends of martial arts novel. His works is fantasy and iconic. Most readers expressed their love for his works. Typically, A people reads a novel takes long times, but people nowdays it's not easy to make time for reading. Those readers has "Enjoy" novels extremly and spend over 3 hour average. They needs frequently action of scrolling swipe down or left during the reading on mobile screen, in which caused unsmoothly reads journey also.

Research and Empathize

Knowing the Novel Readers

Kicking off, I started my research by understanding the problem I’m trying to solve. I planned a quick study with 8 people who reads novel within the target audience, all of whom had experienced before and try to get their thoughts. Asked them questions via Facebook Jin-Yong's novels fans group:

  • Which platform do they use for reading? why printed vesion & why e-Reader?
  • What are the time do they generally read?
  • How much time do they dedicate to reading, How often do they read?
  • Is Reading at home or public transit?
  • Any issue faced when reading novels on current app platforms?

Plus, I assigned some simple tasks to 2 people to see how they would personally use it and identify reading issue.

-  in-App Reading
-  Check Reading status and History
-  Time Spending

Finding out Pain Points & Analysis

Next, I compiled all the Play Store review, testing feedback and result listed above and grouped similar are. The major pattern discovered over 80% of Jin Yong's avid readers, while other 20% are infrequently to read most of the times. From this, I summarized four pain points. which are about:

#1PAIN POINT

Insufficient of in-App reading feature

Over 75% frustrated with limited reading status, only page bookmarking option caused inconvenience due to long period reading especially.

Key takeaway : 
Not enough reading feature doesn't fullfill users situation in which make them less sure how many time spend on novel reads.

#2PAIN POINT

Uncomfortable on mobile reading

Over 50% audiences dislike read novel through mobile, they need long scrolling and swiping on the screen frequently, and also said loss concentration during reading period they felt. Some interviewees said prefered hard-copy and Amazon Kindle reads.

Key takeaway : 
Reading platform doesn't match up with real-life usage scenarios, which means mobile not ideal for read text-heavy novel.

#3PAIN POINT

Lack of Book genres

As most audience say mentioned haven't Jin-Yong's relevant and other categoies at all.

Key takeaway : 
They ending up chose either which of hard-copy version or online ebook store, and also might with chance loss of potential audience opportunity.

#4PAIN POINT

Unattractive UI

Over 40% audiences feels bored: "Ugly", “it doesn't interest me”, "Seem like reading article", especially avid novel readers.

Key takeaway : 
Such a pure of text e-reader had made thier disappointed which would just turn off the audiences interest. Current apps was not up to marks as imagery of Jin Yong's novels.

Hypothesis #2

Tablet-first as Primary Reading Platform

"Fulfilling their need to reads fluently and comfortably"

INSIGHTS :

Jin Yong's novels is a text-heavy fiction. Novel readers would not be rush to reading, because it isn't online article or news. Typically, Mobile not suitable for long period reading. Would not solve the problem even users enlarge font size because mobile screen size was fixed, only causing more scrolling frequently.

RATIONALE :

Tablet landscape screen almost same as hard-copy size of novel that well-adapted Z-pattern for human reading. It is crucial to make them comfortable and fluently when reading on wide screen, and especially a ton of words novels. Hypothesis #3

Persona "MAX" who is Novel Lover

I setup primary persona - MAX, to capture the roadmap of our target users describe how a user might think, do and feel about a certain situation.

Scenarios :

MAX as a busy Media Writer in middle-class, valued his time and work life balance. Due to his constant need of writing skill and idea, He love reading. He contemplates what to read something while busy in works after.

However, With about 2 hour left before sleeping. Before starting reading, he looking through the reading prcoess bar and system evaluate time first, and make decision how much time available to reading, MAX feels helpfull~ so that he can spend time for family and somewhat things in home. Due to the reading on iPad, he feels it comfortable and perfextly fits. So, He can better enjoy the reading time after long day at work.

Hypothesis Statement

"We believe provide more imformative of reading status and comfortable platform for novel readers will increase reading effeiceny, even though the long hours works make it difficult for reading. This is because tend to know more reading info before actually reading, in which helps to easy to make time for reading. Despite mobile usage widely, reading on a tablet is just as enjoyable as reading a printed version novel."

Ideating the Strategy

The Solution

Transform e-book reader into Jin-Yong's Theme-Bookstore

Through reconstructe the reading experience rather than pure of text e-reader, and giving novels readers multiple levels of imagery and valuable, where differentiate from competitors in the market.

In order to give reader a comfortable solution to aid their experience. Approach as "Tablet-first" where wide screen not only concentrate on reading, even a ton of words novels bring the user comfort as well.

#1

Reinforces in-App reading feature

Provide more detail of novels activities that include current reading, charpter info/page and evaluate time for next reading. To do so, users can evaluate that the time spent themselves. Users will be given time aspects control over reading period. Also, By showing percentage bar of current reads that ease way understand rather than page number bookmark.

Apply suitable color scheme for long period reading to reduce eye-stress with text and provides the best legibility. During the research previoulsy, there's no such app that is providing this option for users.

#2

Connect novel reader to build closer relationships

Having categories of about the Jin-Yong's were, such as Jin Studies research (金學) & martial arts relevant book at the Theme-bookstore given opportunity achieve to a broader user group.

A novels visual element arcoss the whole UI to improve ambience of Jin-Yong-themed bookstore, especially presented in Jin-Yong's works was important.

Create value-added content that about the well-known characters and martial arts which allow user interact with. Make it more memorable experience.

The reimagined Jin-Yong’s Themed-Bookstore

A Themed-Bookstore brings freshness landing experience with infinite-looping posterboard. Reinforced in-App reading experience

In "My Bookshelf" where shows overview of novel. With status bar in current novel section. User can keep track novel reading status that contain: Current reading, Evaluate time, Percentage of pages reads, Last reading time, and Chapter/pages detail. Those data updated instanly when done reading. Allowing users can evaluate that the time spent themselves, and given time aspects control over reading period clearly. open_in_new Detail

Within in-App reader, User able to open user preference setting panel by top right button. With swiping quick access button, its direct jump to page. Moreover, There are variety of option which is customize option to suit users reading behavior, such as font face, size, colors, night mode, page turn interactions and bookmarks with auto-saving the currently etc. open_in_new Detail

As prototyping shown, the Tablet-first approach was smoothly and comfortable reading with wide screen, which is feel less cramped as well as better for novels reads. The novels illustrations between the chapter was well presented that keep feel "Real" same as printed version. open_in_new Detail

Suitable color scheme help for eye strain

"Eye protect mode" as new color scheme with a specific for reading long period. A background color value as #f4f4ec and gray font as #333, with a bit emboss paper texture same as printed version. This combination of color scheme was give out a soft and natural. It would have been useful to less of a stimulating effect on our eyes. That provides natural contrast and readability for body text. open_in_new Detail
