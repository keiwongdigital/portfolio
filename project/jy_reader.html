Jin-Yong's martial arts novels bookstore
Are you a martial arts novels junkie? If yes, probably you are no stranger to Jin Yong.
Jin-Yong's is the pen name of Dr Cha Leung-yung, world bestselling martial arts novelist. His works elevated the popular martial arts novel to an artistic level. Were read by generations of ethnic Chinese, which inspired film, television, comic book and video game adaptations.

Most people said: "凡有華人的地方，就有人認識金庸"
Duration 4 Month
Scope Tablet APP
Role UX task / UI Design

Goals

Through a new platform of Themed-Bookstore to reconstruct the reading experience for Jin Yong martial arts novels reader. Provide comfortable and smoothly way of fulfilling their hobby in reading.

  • Usability - Propose a more engaging experience so that users are comfortable accessing novel
  • Consistency - Establish a visual language to optimize overall Themed-Bookstore
  • Functionality - Offers a better reading experience with its extensive features.

Role

This was an individual project. Though I am not in the publishing business, I did however like the idea of re-designing Jin-Yong novels e-reader. I take full of the roles involved in designing a product UX and UI.

Research

Here, I did an app audit to analyze and understand the ebook reader app overall navigation, features and its usabilities as well. Throughout this process, I was able to identify some clear usability issues and pain points. Thus, in order to validate these observations, I needed to perform some user interviews and testings and try to get their thoughts.

First off, I conducted interviews adnd testing the app with 8 people (who active, infrequent e-Reader and printed version as well) by What's App. To understanding they faced problem and behavior patterns.

Then, I assigned some simple tasks to see how they would personally use it:

  • 1) Navigation around App
  • 2) In-App Reading diff scenes: Home / Public transit
  • 3) Time Spend
  • 4) Check your reading status and history

After through affinity mapping quickly, I attempted to further group the problem to come up for most pain points and emotional feels. Here are summarized user quotes at below:

  • Incomplete reader feature
    All of them didn’t know where to check. They said that not having a reading status or history caused inconvenience especially long time reading users.
  • Reading platform
    Uncomfortable reading on limited screen of mobile that most obvious comment of printed users. 5 people who said reads at MTR has interference and shoulder pain. 1 people who said Amazon Kindle with better devices for reading.
  • Lack of book genres
    3/8 of them said that not provided of Jin-Yong's and relevant novels products and interesting content.
  • Simple and outdated UI
    6/8 of them said that boring layout and unappealing, especially avid readers mentioned "It's not catching for me".

Key Takeaways:

  • PROBLEM #1 - Every readers has a own personalized reading habits. No having a tracking features caused problems of lower thier reading efficiency and flexibility which most mentioned. Thus, users desire a traceable information and history what current reading, and how long to time spend in reading.
  • PROBLEM #2 - Long period on mobile screen cause thier uncomfortable and concern health issue as well. Avid readers has "Enjoy" novels extremly and spend 2.5+ hour average. Thus, users desire to better enviorment and devices to read more fluently. It ended up why trend to printed version.
  • PROBLEM #3 - They perfered to discover novels storyline and martial arts book after they have read novels. There is no specific relevant or book they wanted. Thus, They trend to search on goolge.
  • PROBLEM #4 - Interface and visual design that can not get users interested. Jin Yong's is legends of martial arts novel. Some of avid readers expressed their love for his works. Lack of novels element had made thier disappointed.

Develop the Themed-Bookstore

During the discovery phase which inspired me to create a themed bookstore. With that in mind, I determined to transform the e-book reader into a Jin-Yong's theme bookstore where through reconstructe the whole experience and gain value as well. During user research phase, I identified that the approach of themed bookstore was aligning well with the acheive users goals concurrently. Hoping that it helps to fit reading into thier lives. Moreover, which able to bring the wide range of printed potential audience to the digital.

#1 Enhance In-App reader feature

Reading status tracking as key desirable functions during the user research. Lets help the users meets thier reading behaviour or unexpected cause stoped reading. It’s been studied that being able to track progress is a great source of motivation. Seeing only a page number can be demotivating. Keeping track of an increasing percentage makes even the thickest of novels more approachable; Let users feel like achieving something each reading. This is especially to achieve goals who want to manage time effectively and multitasks users as well. They trend to spend time with the personal things mostly.

In order to improve the reading experience and make it more personal and customizable, I created a "Protect" mode and "Reading in-printed" theme which are users reads comfortably on long period and feel like "Real" emotional needs as well.

#2 Customised book by genres

New category where the user can find "About Jin-Yong’s", “Jin studies” and novels research book etc other than novels.That would be expand audience reach and driven the purchase.

#3 Value-added content

Interactive section that about the well-known characters and martial arts which are main element of the Jin-Yong novels. This makes the bookstore more attractive and user engagement greatly.

#4 Consistency with visual designs

Visual whole stage belongs to Jin-Yong's novels element. I outlined 3 keywords: Legends, History and Martial arts. These are necessary elements of the novels taht it would be improve ambience of Jin-Yong-themed bookstore.

Usability of platform

Normally, The novels with a ton of words and much pages, such as most popular "射鵰英雄傳" with 1.2 million words. It will take you much longer to finish the novel.

Discovery users needs and observation of real life situations during the user feedback. I intend to design with a "Tablet-first" approach that bring the user optimal of usability, comfortable experience and health issues is important as well. The tablet scale screen not only lowers abandonment rates, but can also concentrate on reading. Landscape orientation almost same as physical hard-copy size.

Although mobile used widely, but single-handed opreate that not ideal for long time reading, especially a ton of words novels. It was inefficient and time-consuming. User needs frequently action of scrolling swipe down or swipe left during reading process.

IDEATION

User Flow

Before I started sketching the wireframes, I created a user flow of how the newly redesigned features would connect with each other and how the users would navigate and use them.

Sketch ideas

I started fast idea sketching to see how we can solve these problems more easily with different ideas on a paper prototype.

Visual

I decided three keywords for rollCase entire visual apperenance of the app: Realistic, Stylish and Professional.

The app is image-driven including a lot of digital cards and users avatars. Use dark background makes the digital cards visuals deeper and makes the content highly legible with the orange accent color. To improve user experience, usage huge set of icon can help users navigate and operate the app clearly and quickly. In the type color, I used white for the text and and included a great deal of grey description to give a calm and clean appearance.

Additionally, I hope take that for a good balance of usability and visual appeal. Design approaches varous engaging illustration elements to keep it fun while working.

Iterations

During testing the prototype phase, I fixed some accessibility issues. Also, simple A/B testing with interviewers where compare two versions of a app flow in order to optimize a certain result or metric and find out which of them is more effective especially paper card convert to digital card process. After that, I conducted prototype using XD which improved the interaction feedback and made the prototype feel more realistic.
