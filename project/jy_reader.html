Jin-Yong's Novels Themed-bookstore
Are you a martial arts novels avid? If yes, probably you are no stranger to Jin Yong.
Jin-Yong's is the pen name of Dr Cha Leung-yung, world bestselling martial arts novelist. His 15 works elevated the popular martial arts novel to an artistic level, and even generating a sort of common language and iconic.

Most people said: "凡有華人的地方，就有人認識金庸".
Deliverables Tablet App
Scope UX - Strategy, Research, Ideation / UI - Visual Design, Prototyping

Project Goals

Develop an Themed-Bookstore experience that centred around Jin-Yong's novels ambience. Aimed to user-centred approached that was fulfilling novels lover reading habit, and building relationships between martial arts novels and audiences deeply.

This was an individual project started from my design challenge and own experience. His novels helped people inspire their fantasies in mind, including me. So i tried to improve the overall experience of the app.

Problem Statements

Existing novels reader app were not portraying value and negative experience

Dr. Jin Yong's is legends of martial arts novel. His works is fantasy and iconic. Most readers expressed their love for his works. Pure of text e-reader and lack of novels element had made thier disappointed. Typically, A people reads a novel takes long times, but people nowdays it's not easy to make time for reading. Those readers has "Enjoy" novels extremly and spend over 3 hour average. They needs frequently action of scrolling swipe down or left during the reading on mobile screen, which caused eye strain occurs and poor reading experience also.

Discovery and Empathize

Knowing the Novel Readers

Kicking off, I started my research by understanding the problem I’m trying to solve. I planned a quick study with 8 people who reads novels within the target audience, and try to get their thoughts. Asked them questions like:

  • Which platform do they use for reading? why (if) physical & why e-Reader?
  • What are the time do they generally read?
  • How much time do they dedicate to reading, How often do they read?
  • Any issue faced when reading novels on current app platforms?

Plus, I assigned some simple tasks to 2 people to see how they would personally use it and identify reading issue.

-  Navigating entire App
-  Check reading status and history
-  Time spending
-  Reading at Home / Public transit

Identifying pain points and finding out reading patterns

Next, I compiled all the Playstore review, testing feedback and result listed above and grouped similar are. The major pattern discovered is 90% of Jin Yong's avid readers, while other 10% are infrequently to read most of the times. Apart from that, almost everyone is frustrated with lack of reading activity. From this, I summarized four pain points. which are about:

  • Insufficient of Reading status
  • Uncomfortable on mobile reading
  • Limited Categories
  • Unattractive Interface

In-depth understanding

Next, it is essential to specified prioritize the goal factor and who I am designing for. I analyzed the insights through a hypothesis process and provide a basis for decision-making going forward.

Hypothesis : Novel readers needed a detail status of reading

Most people who often multitasking, in which assumed that readers would want to do other activities like playing games or somewhat on social media while reading, Only a Bookmark feature can be demotivating and lower thier reading efficiency and motivation. Hypothesis : Tablet-first as Reading Platform

Jin Yong's novels is a text-heavy fiction. Novel readers would not be rush to reading, because it isn't online article or news. Mobile not suitable for long period reading. Would not solve the problem even users enlarge font size because mobile screen size was fixed, causing more horizontal scrolling frequently also.

Rationale :

A tablet landscape screen almost same as physical hard-copy size that well-adapted Z-pattern people reading. It is crucial to make them comfortable and fluently when reading on wide screen, and especially a ton of words novels. Across those interviewed, they preferred to reading novels with printed version at most, and would typically rather reading them at home. Of course, that could be loss mobile user base engaging. But there’s no such thing as a perfect design/devices. Below are the point that supports the "Tablet-first" assumptions i analysed:

Ideating the Strategy

Solution

Theme-Bookstore Approaching

Transform the e-book reader app came up to a Jin-Yong's theme bookstore where through reconstructe the whole experience and giving novels readers multiple levels of imagery and feelings. In order to give people a comfortable solution to aid their experience. Approach as "Tablet-first" where wide screen not only concentrate on reading and eye health issues, even a ton of words novels bring the user comfort as well.

Reinforces novels reading status

  • Show a variety of Novel activity that include current reading, charpter info/page and evaluate time for next reading. Users will be given time aspects control over which take time for current reading and which available time of reading. Also, By showing percentage of current reads that ease way understand rather than page number.
  • Apply suitable color scheme for long period reading to reduce eye-stress with text and provides the best legibility.

Connect novel reader to build closer relationships

  • Having categories of about the Jin-Yong's relevant Jin Studies (金學) & martial arts relevant book at the Theme-bookstore given opportunity achieve to a broader user group.
  • A novels visual element arcoss the whole UI to improve ambience of Jin-Yong-themed bookstore, especially presented in Jin-Yong's works was important.
  • Create value-added content that about the well-known characters and martial arts which allow user interact with. Make it more memorable experience.

Crafting the Visual

Design System

Whole stage belongs to Jin-Yong's novels element. Here, I outlined 2 keywords: "Originality" and "Iconic". These are necessary elements of the novels, which ideating to design App logo also.

Overall UI design is simple and emotional to establish a connection. Since the pages are going to be filled with many book covers, it is ideal to keep the app colors as minimal as possible. A light bit yellow as background colors while wine red as accent colors. Besides, I've also customize four set of book mockup and a unique vector calligraphy element as well. It make the message and interactions clealy.

Digital Illustration

Personally, I am impressed with the infinite possibilities that Jin-Yong’s novels offer. So, I did spend time thinking too much about the visual. I decided to create something which idea from the novels and imbue them with my own feelings. For this purpose, I created martial arts and storyline of novels illustration as innovative visual elements as well as consistent with the whole UI.

The reimagined Jin-Yong’s Themed-Bookstore

A Themed-Bookstore approach was completely providing a fresh experience. With dynamic infinite-loop posterboard navigation, I deliberately martial arts illustration as entry page to relate to their respective section, which can also allows to keep readers interested through the entire navigation that evoke emotions immediately and make it more delightful experience.

As prototyping shown, The "Bookstore" screen where divided into 3 category that shows :《細讀巨匠》 Jin-Yong’s Novels Collections, 《江湖以外》 Novels reference book and 《論盡金學》 Jin Studies research book. open_in_new Bookstore

Reinforced in-App reading experience

Upon this demo, "My Bookshelf" screen shows overview of novel. User can keep track of reading status that contain what novel current reading, percentage of pages reads, last reading time and chapter/pages detail. No longer need to assume how much time it will take to reading. The app will evaluate a novels that take time for next time reading so that users can easily compare with thier available time. This would be shown to users every time they reads done and update current reading progress instanly. open_in_new My Bookshelf

Within in-App reader, User able to open user preference setting panel by top right button. With swiping quick access button, its direct jump to page. Moreover, There are variety of option which is customize option to suit users reading behavior, such as font face, size, colors, night mode, page turn interactions and bookmarks with auto-saving the currently etc. open_in_new User preference panel

As prototyping shown, the Tablet-first approach was smoothly and comfortable reading with wide screen, which is feel less cramped as well as better for novels reads. The novels illustrations between the chapter was well presented that keep feel "Real" same as printed version. open_in_new in-App Reader

Suitable color scheme help for eye strain

With "Eye protect mode" as new color scheme with a specific for reading long period. A background color value as #f4f4ec and gray font as #333, with a bit emboss paper texture same as printed version. This combination of color scheme was give out a soft and natural. It would have been useful to less of a stimulating effect on our eyes. That provides natural contrast and readability for body text.

Credit: The color scheme according to "Luz Rello - Bigham Human-Computer Interaction Institute & Carnegie Mellon University"

Extra novels value-added content
