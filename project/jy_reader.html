Jin-Yong's Martial Arts Novels Themed-bookstore
Are you a martial arts novels avid? If yes, probably you are no stranger to Jin Yong.
Jin-Yong's is the pen name of Dr Cha Leung-yung, world bestselling martial arts novelist. His works elevated the popular martial arts novel to an artistic level. Were read by generations of ethnic Chinese and still was favorites in the Chinese-speaking world, which inspired film, television, comic book and video game adaptations.

Reading martial arts novels is about building relationships with authors, worlds, characters, and stories. Most people said: "凡有華人的地方，就有人認識金庸".
Deliverables Tablet App
Scope UX - Strategy, Research, Ideation / UI - Visual Design, Prototyping

Project Goals

Propose more engaging experience by establishing Jin-Yong's novels Themed-Bookstore, instead of e-Reader app only. Offer user-relevant features that bring delight fulfilling their hobby in reading between novels and audiences.

This was an individual passion project based on my own experience. Though I am not in the digital publishing field, I did however like the idea of re-designing Jin-Yong novels e-Reader. I take full of the roles involved in designing a product UX and UI.

Problem Statement

"How might I better convey the value of the Jin-Yong's novels ebook reader, bring readers experiencing of Jin-Yongs novels comfortably and enjoyable simultaneously?"

DISCOVERY

Research

Kicking off the project, I did an app analyzes and understand in the current market, which include navigation, features and its usability as well. Then, in order to validate these observations, I needed to perform some user interviews and experience with e-Reader. and try to get their thoughts. I conducted interviews about the app with 8 people (who reads digitally and printed version) using Facebook group vote poll. Plus, I assigned some simple tasks to see how they would personally use it and reading behavior patterns.

Task 1 : Navigating entire App
Task 2 : in-App Reading on difference scenes: Home / Public transit
Task 3 : Time Spend
Task 4 : Check reading status and history

Next, After I through affinity mapping quickly, I attempted to further group the problem to come up for major problems. Here are summarized by user quotes at below:

Pain Point

  • Incomplete e-Reader feature
    All of them didn’t know where to check reading status of novels thier reads. They said that not having a track detail or history caused inconvenience especially long period reading users and want to evaluate time. With 2/8 mentioned the some function error or crash also.
  • Reading Device
    Uncomfortable reading on mobile screen is most comment users. They need long scrolling and swiping on the screen frequently. With 5 people who said reads at MTR has interference and shoulder pain. With 3 people who mentioned Amazon Kindle wide screen for well reading.
  • Lack of Book genres
    4/8 of them said Jin-Yong's novels relevant book and content they desired. Endup they googling.
  • Ugly Interface
    6/8 of them said that the app unattractive, especially avid readers mentioned "Boring, Like reading article"; "It's not catching for me".

Understanding user what are they feeling and thinking

POINT #1

Every readers has a own personalized reading habits. No having a tracking features caused most frustrating things. The impact of lower thier reading efficiency and motivation they mentioned. Thus, they wanted a traceable information and history what current reading, and how long to time spend in reading, causing in app crashes and multitask audiences especially.

POINT #2

Long period on mobile screen cause thier uncomfortable and concern health issue as well. Avid readers has "Enjoy" novels extremly and spend 2.5+ hour average. Hence, users desire to better devices and enviorment to read more fluently. So, they ended up why trend to printed version. That's why the users perfer physical book over e-book.

POINT #3

They perfered to discover novels relevant book and martial arts info after they have read novels. But there haven't relevant provided. Thus, they endup to another e-book store to purchase they wanted.

POINT #4

Overall look and feel that can not get users interested. Jin Yong's is legends of martial arts novel. Some of avid readers expressed their love for his works. Lack of novels element and chapter illustration had made thier disappointed.

SOLUTION

So how do I think solutions?
And what problem are i trying to solve?

Developing the Themed-Bookstore

During the discovery phase which inspired me to create a themed bookstore. With that in mind, I determined to transform the e-book reader app came up to a Jin-Yong's theme bookstore where through reconstructe the whole experience and gain value as well. During the result of user research phase, I identified that the approach of themed bookstore was aligning well with the acheive users goals concurrently. This approach giving readers multiple levels of imagery and feelings to enhance the reading experience and align with avid readers needs.

  • Provide more Book genre and Value-added content
    By adding book genre which Jin-Yong, such as “Jin Studies (金學)” and novels research book to expand audience reach. Additionaly, By creating interactive value-added content that about the well-known characters and martial arts which are main element of the Jin-Yong novels, that resonate with target audiences expectation.
  • Enrich ambience of Theme-bookstore
    By createing digital illustrations intergated into UI gives users fantasy and a bit gamified. It would be improve ambience of Jin-Yong themed-bookstore, fulfill with physical printed users base also.

Enhance Utility of in-App Reader

Reading status tracking is functions they desire for during the user research. This is key to help the users meets thier reading behaviour or unexpected cause stoped reading. It’s been studied that being able to track progress is a great source of motivation. Seeing only a page number can be demotivating. By showing percentage that ease way understand rather than page number, Also, evaluate a novels that take time for next time reading also to reduce user stress. Makes it even the thickest of novels more friendly; This is especially to achieve goals who want to manage time effectively and multitasks users as well. They trend to spend time with the personal things mostly.

Plus, Interviewees mentioned that when reads long time mostly feels eye strain. So, I decided to find color scheme for suitable reading how to reduce body text visual stress and the best legibility.

Reading Platform Strategy

Suitable devices is one of key they are looking for. According to users real life situations, Read novels in home as most usage scenarios and motivations. I intend to design with a "Tablet-first" approach where landscape screen almost same as physical hard-copy size to bring the user optimal of utility and comfortable reading experience. The tablet scale screen not only lowers abandonment rates and eye health issues, but can also concentrate on reading. Below are the picked data that supports the "Tablet-first" approach:

VISUAL IDENTITY

Design System

One consistent of feedback from former and avid users, and across both interviews and testing, is that Jin-Yong's novels reader existing visual design is dated and aesthetically unappealing. Due to this, I decided that creating a uniqueness visual identity and more intuitive user experience as well.

Visual whole stage belongs to Jin-Yong's novels element. I outlined 3 keywords: "Legends", "History" and "Martial arts". These are necessary elements of the novels, which ideating to design App logo also.

The overall UI design is simple and emotional to establish a connection. Since the pages are going to be filled with many book covers, it is ideal to keep the app colors as minimal as possible. A light bit yellow as background colors while wine red as accent colors. I've also customize four set of book mockup and a unique vector calligraphy element as well. It make the message and interactions clealy.

Digital Illustration

Personally, I am impressed with the infinite possibilities that Jin-Yong’s novels offer. So, I did spend ton of time thinking too much about the visual element. In order to present storyline, I decided to create something which idea from the novels and imbue them with my own feelings. For this purpose, i created martial arts and storyline novels illustration as innovative visual elements and consistent with the whole UI.

A fresh viewpoint on Themed-Bookstore

This approach was completely providing a fresh viewpoint on landing experience. With Infinite-loop posterboard navigation, I deliberately chose each illustration tap area to relate to their respective section, which allows to keep people interested through the entire navigation.

With bookstore page which categorized content where sperated into the three category of book, ‘細讀巨匠’ as Novels collections, ‘江湖以外’ as Reference book and ‘論盡金學’ as Research book with horizontal scrolling for groups of book views. A rest of the page is reserved for specific offer and popular books banner etc.

Clearly informative of Reading activity

In "My Bookshelf" page allow the user can keep track of reading status easily that contain of currently, percentage of pages reads, last reading time and chapter/pages detail. No longer need to assume how much time it will take to reading. The app will evaluate a novels that take time for next time reading. This would be shown to users every time they reads done and update current reading progress instanly. Also, shown a history of reading list with progress bar.

Reinforces the In-App Reading experience

As prototyping shown, the Tablet-first approach was smoothly and comfortable reading with wide screen and the whole UI feel less cramped as well as better for eyes friendly experience, which is visually clean and legible. The novels illustrations between the chapter was reserved that keep feel "Real" same as printed version.

Quick swiping button with detail info make it clearly what a page shown when users swiping. Moreover, user able to open user preference setting panel by top right button. There are variety of option which is customize option, such as font face, size, colors, night mode, page turn interactions and bookmarks with auto-saving the currently etc to suit users reading behavior.

Suitable option help for reading long period

The "Eye protect mode" as new color scheme. A background color value as #555F61 and gray font as #333, with a bit emboss paper texture same as printed version. This combination of color scheme was give out a soft and natural which has less of a stimulating effect on our eyes. That provides natural contrast, optimal readability and comfortable for body text.

Credit: The color scheme according to "Luz Rello - Bigham Human-Computer Interaction Institute & Carnegie Mellon University"

Extra value-added content

With a new section where are interactive content allow user can see the novels relevant information that contains well-known characters, as well as a list of martial arts knowledge and make the user experience more rewarding and enriched theme-bookstore image.

TASK COMPLETION RATE:

80%

Task 2: User Reading Activity

TASK COMPLETION RATE:

85%

Result: "There is clearly infomation, I could keep track my status and history, make me aware use of time and effort when i was reading." "Nice, It also calculates the reading time"

Task 3: In-App Reader

TASK COMPLETION RATE:

78%

Result: "This app suits reading really well, I think I’ll use tablet reading further; Eye-protect mode was helful for me, i felt smooth on reading"

Participants has responded has a positive feedback and satisfaction rate by the bookstore approach compare with ebook readers. In terms of the in-App Reader, Reading on Tablet reader can be really fullfilling their reading behaviors and expectations. Of course, that could be loss mobile user base engaging. But there’s no such thing as a perfect design/devices. However, would have great conversion rate in place i noted.
