Universal Music Group (Hong Kong)
Universal Music Group Hong Kong is a division of Universal Music Group. Universal Music Group is the world leader in music-based entertainment, with a broad array of businesses engaged in recorded music, music publishing, merchandising and audiovisual content in more than 60 countries.

UMG current website are outdated and non-mobile friendly. These days, audience's first impression of your business is based almost exclusively on digital platform why itʼs so important to have a digital marketing strategy.
Client Universal Music Group HK Branch
Scope Web Design
Role UI Design / UX task

Objective

In partnership with UMG Hong Kong. This project aimed to provide affiliate contents platform that satisfy fans by promote physical and digital albums. That will improve user engagement and trust also helped thier digital Marketing strategy. Increase online and physical sales, return visits, brand supporting and audience interaction through the affiliate marketing platform.

The Affiliate marketing relationship of UMG: 

  • Affiliate Creator - UMG who provide product and content
  • Affiliate Marketer - We provide platform and create product tracking links, commonly known as affiliate links in productsʼ detail page will direct consumers to UMGʼs official payment website (UShop.com) to checkout.

Goals

Based on our initial conversation with UMG what pain points actually are, the following goals decided upon:

  • Re-align website with their digital marketing strategy
  • Simple and intuitive user-friendly experience
  • Strong visual website is needed

Role

Over the project of 3 months, I was part of the core team. I focused on renew UI visual design and part of front-end coding ensure the best outcome. As the only designer in this project collaborating closely with product managers, develop teammate, UMG marketing team and stakeholders to develop the new platform.

Define

During it's intial stages, Identifying their current website problem and UMG most concerned how to reach thier business goals through affiliate platforms. In addition, to gave us to define the logical, content structure, processes and functionalities of the platforms. Base on goals defined, The solution are:

  • Keep it simple and this will help design feel structured. Make it easy for visitors to explore and better experiences
  • Embedded social feed wall on website can help the client no need publish a new content also keep it fresh
  • Maintain easy paths for buyers to purchase album online
  • Visually appealing website to keep visitors as long as possible
  • Responsive design across all device was needed

Design Approach

01_Ideation

First, I sketching some user journey and content architecture which helped us quickly get an idea and give a rough concepts. We realized some of the ideas and layouts that we were thinking of just wouldn't work.

02_Wireframeing

After determined the flow and team deliberation. I translated into low-fi wireframes from inital sketch to build the quick look and key features and user flow to gather initial feedback from the team. Out of that, to make sure every flow was complete and the tasks order were logical which could more discussions, intial test, iterate on the outcome and annotated them.

03_Design Guide

Once we collected core content, I validated the correct usage of their brand guidelines and asset library. Then, created mood boards on Photoshop where algin pins of style, typography, and color schemes that aligned with my vision for UMG Hong Kong.

Also, designing the interface considering principles such as brand attributes, hierarchy, visual illustration and team feedback. I approached the whole visual concept was simple, unique and attractive.

Grid

Describe all blocks of the web site pages and blocks elements. Modular Grid approach is that it makes visual design and CSS to gel well and responsive design. It involves how to use the space efficiently, and what the size ratio of all the elements

Typeface

As for the typeface is important. I chose a font that has readability as its primarily characteristic and comfortable reading experience. Finally selected two typefaces: Noto Sans and Open Sans that also gives consistency both Chinese and English

Color

Approach dark themed which can express a wider range of color and depth. Also enhance album cover contrast which important for the music site. Used high gradient contrast and bright color for CTA button stand out from the background.

Customize Visual

Create a set of calligraphy visual to whole website are important to make the users anchored. These visual can be used in several places such as section title, album detail, bio making the characteristic of the website more coherent

04_Visual Language

From this phase, I moved onto high-fidelity prototyping, Need to think and reassemble the contents to keep things consistent across all the elements in the new design. I made sure the design would something very simple. The landing page would say it all and as user scroll down, they would exactly know what this whole website is about.


Then we determined a new visual direction after a round of usability testing. I dedicated time and effort in designing each UI modular which its associated user interaction to ensure easy usability. In addtion, creating a component library which flexible for reusable as the website grows and also provide a highly efficient way to build the site.

Takeaway

The project was very big and fun for me. I learnt the role visual design plays in reflecting the mission and values of a music production brand. It is so important to spend time developing the visual design aesthetics. As a designer, it's not just screen design, viewing the product with the holistic view from various angles has been a great help that it represent what the brand stood for and what it wanted to project to its customers.

Another key takeaway from working on this project was realizing how to best prepare the assets, design elements and system so that the teammate could understand easily and systematically, reducing the amount of time for back-and-forth communication and adjustment.

The whole experiencing opened my eyes to the difficulties and joys of creating digital products.
