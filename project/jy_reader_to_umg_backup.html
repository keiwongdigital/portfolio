Context

Universal Music Group. is the world leader in music-based entertainment, with a broad array of businesses engaged in recorded music, music publishing, merchandising and audiovisual content in more than 60 countries. Nowdays, audience's first impression of your business is based almost exclusively on digital platform why itʼs so important to create a digital marketing strategy.

In a affiliate program partnership with UMG Hong Kong, Here are the role of relationship:

  • Affiliate Creator - UMG who provide product and content
  • Affiliate Marketer- Establishing platform that through an affiliate tracking links in album detail page will direct consumers to UMGʼs official payment website (UShop.com) to checkout.

Project Goals

  • Redefine the ways in which content can be discovered and consumed within the website in order to increase engagement and dwell time.
  • Evolve the brand elements to create a recognisable and congruent visual language.
  • Elevate the overall experience to align with the iconic of the brand and the quality of content it produces.

Role

DISCOVERY

Problem

According to several meeting with UMG what business goals actually are. This gives us a better idea to work toward.

Because of the short time frame and scope of the project. We conducted the user interviews rapidly, Then, we started to evaluate the current website to identify what the main problems their are facing. Here are key finding:

  • Poor organization, unclear information structure and has an excessive amount of pages by the sub-menu. Poor search function return incomplete result and return wrong result
  • Whole website was outdated and inconsistency of branding that affect users when they are shopping online intent
  • Website non Mobile-friendly that would deliver poor experience and feeling frustrated. also hurts website SEO ranking as well
  • Missing social content not able to reach target audience attention and get them engaged

Competitive Analysis

In this phase, We are researched current market music brand would set them apart from their competitors website and better convey UMG business goal.

The majority of the features between competitiors were very similar, however the main differences that we noticed were:

  • Easily Accessible vs Hardly Accessible
  • Too Many Screens vs Simplified Interaction
  • Distracting Interface vs Minimalistic Interface
  • Visually of Products
