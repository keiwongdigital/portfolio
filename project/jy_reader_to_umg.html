Project Goals

The project aimed to create the affiliate marketing program which connections between offical website and thier standalone payment system and redesign current website simultaneously that helps UMG Hong Kong achieve bussiness goals through optimize brand online image and increase engagement.

  • Redefine the ways in which content can be discovered and consumed within the website in order to increase engagement and dwell time.
  • Evolve the brand elements to create a recognisable and congruent visual language.
  • Elevate the overall experience to align with the iconic of the brand and the quality of content it produces.

In a affiliate program partnership with UMG Hong Kong, Here are the role of relationship:

  • Affiliate Creator - UMG who provide product and content
  • Affiliate Marketer- Establishing platform that through an affiliate tracking links in album detail page will direct consumers to UMGʼs official payment website (UShop.com) to checkout.

Role

DISCOVERY

Problem

In order to bring the better experience and to match the business goals, the initial research helped us get the right insights on user behaviour, target groups and the structure of the website from audenice and brand point of view.

Because of the short time frame and scope of the project. We conducted the user interviews rapidly, Then, we started to evaluate the current website to identify what the main problems their are facing. Here are finding:

  • Poor content organization, unclear information structure and has an excessive amount of pages by the sub-menu
  • There is no option to search within a specific, such as news article or detail, return incomplete result
  • Whole website was outdated and inconsistency of branding that affect users when they are shopping online intent
  • Website non Mobile-friendly that would deliver poor experience and decrease webiste traffic. Moreover, hurts SEO ranking as well
  • Missing social content not able to aligns with key user needs and get them engaged

Competitive Analysis

In this phase, We are researched current market music brand would set them apart from their competitors website and better convey UMG business goal.

The majority of the features between competitiors were very similar, however the main differences that we noticed were:

  • Easily Accessible vs Hardly Accessible
  • Too Many Screens vs Simplified Interaction
  • friendly Interface vs Complexity Interface
  • Visually of Products

Information Architecture

In order to create more organized content and focus to the main goals, We are reorganized the content architecture, combined the duplicated section. And give a new name to the old section. I sketched out the simple wireflow which helped us quickly get an idea and give a rough concepts. I tried to avoid the unapplicable design based on front-end developing skill.

There are now five section in the revised which are ‘HOME’ ‘UMG PRESS’ ‘GENRE’ ‘MEDIA’ ‘ABOUT’ ‘CONTACT’.

Avoid deep navigation, I created new ‘UMG Press’ section contain as sub tag: ‘焦點新聞’ is where as a UMG official news. ‘藝人社交’ is where embeded social wall feature that can find all the latest update on artist SNS . ‘注目動態’ is artist upcoming event on SNS, such as facebook live and concert content.

Due to time constraints, I skipp the wireframing phase, jumping straight into visual stage and high-fidelity prototyping. This will also spend much time for the visual stage.

Final Thoughts

The project was very big and fun for me. As a designer, it's not just screen design. I learnt the role visual design plays in reflecting the mission and values of a music production brand. It’s extremely important to strike a balance between brand image and actual visual design.

The clients have a vision. But they don't always have the UX knowledge to understand users. Sometimes their requirements conflict with users needs. It's our designers' roles to help them empathize with users and bridge their vision with users' needs, and get on-board with a holistic design solution.
