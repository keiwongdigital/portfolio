2nd Burger House Brand Identity
2nd is a Singapore handmade burger house only open night hour (6:30~4:00), Extremely for night people. Started as a small restaurant at city of Chin Bee. The client needed a branding project to convey the visual of the merge between rustic and modern that the restaurant wanted to transmit to it's costumers.
Deliverables Re-Branding, Instagram Campaigns
Role Logo Design, Brand Identity, Visual Design

Description

The main idea of the logo was to use a hand drawing burger outline that got people's attention and still transmit the brand values, Using bold style font contrast was perfect for it.

"Relax" but conscious and caring for the product, has set the standard for the new places that offer this type of food in the city. I wanted to reflect the casual, fun and pop part of eating a hamburger, also emphasize the care with which the product is made.
